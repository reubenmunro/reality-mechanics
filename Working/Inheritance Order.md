# Inheritance (Working)

> Working document. Not canon.
>
> Inheritance recovers where a generated mechanic continues through a family. It follows Generation. It does not create ontology.

## Three reads

Generation asks:

> What mechanic does this primitive keep making recoverable?

Inheritance asks:

> Where does that mechanic continue, directly or ancestrally?

Dependency asks:

> What must already be true for this term to hold?

Generation, inheritance, and dependency may coincide. They are not assumed to be the same.

## Lens distinction

Generation names the mechanic a source condition makes available.

Inheritance names the continuation of that mechanic through later terms.

Dependency names the immediate supports that must remain true for a term to hold.

A term may:

- depend immediately on one condition,
- inherit ancestrally from another,
- and participate in a generated mechanic that began earlier than either immediate support.

## Lineage reads

Immediate inheritance:

- The nearest recoverable mechanic a term unfolds from.

Ancestral inheritance:

- A deeper mechanic still recognisable through intervening terms.

Dependency:

- The support that must remain present for the term to hold.

Determination pressure:

- A generated mechanic becoming more locally specified, active, sustained, settled, returned, or otherwise changed in depth.
- Not yet a confirmed fourth read.
- Record as pressure until repeated across multiple chains.

Do not collapse these.

## Discipline

Inheritance is not taxonomy.

Taxonomy groups terms by likeness.

Inheritance recovers the mechanic a family continues to carry from its generator.

A term may depend on one condition while inheriting a mechanic from another.

## Recovery rule

Recover upward before outward.

1. Recover the generator.
2. Recover the mechanic.
3. Test where the mechanic continues.
4. Distinguish immediate inheritance, ancestral inheritance, dependency, and determination pressure.
5. Recover the family.
6. Only then recalibrate individual notes.

If Generation remains open, Inheritance remains provisional.

## Current pressure lines

Relation
-> placeability / structural answerability
-> Carrier terms may inherit through Connection and Hold rather than directly.

Boundary
-> localisation / located distinction
-> Availability / Strain / Bearing / Resolution currently read as determination depth rather than one flat family.

Availability
-> presentability / enterability
-> Presence may localise availability; Participation may activate a present bearing relation rather than inherit Availability directly.

Connection
-> passage
-> Carry / Trace / Carrying / Recarry
-> Coupling may be later mutual availability rather than direct Connection inheritance.

These lines are pressure lines for recovery.

# Relation -> Carrier family

Generator:

- Relation

Generated mechanic:

- Open. Current pressure from Generation: Relation may generate placeability / structural answerability more directly than the whole Carrier family.

Immediate inheritance:

- Place appears immediately held by Relation.
- Carry and Trace appear immediately generated by Connection as directional passage.

Ancestral inheritance:

- Carry and Trace may still inherit Relation ancestrally through Connection and Hold.

Dependencies:

- Carry and Trace depend on Hold and Connection.
- Place depends on Relation.

Determination pressure:

- Open.
- Relation has not yet produced a clear determination-depth chain comparable to Boundary or Connection.

Appears through:

- Hold
- Carry
- Trace
- Read
- Place

Observed reads:

- Hold and Carry show dependency without sequence.
- Carry and Trace read forward and backward availability through held connection.
- Relation directly carries Place.
- Connection directly carries Carry and Trace.

Pressure:

- Carrier terms may be note-grammar operators as well as inherited mechanics.
- Trace, Read, and Place need to be checked as inherited mechanics, not merely note-grammar operations.
- Relation -> Carrier is too compressed if Carry/Trace are immediately generated by Connection.

Must recover:

- What from Relation remains recognisable in every carrier term.
- Whether Carrier family should split into Place / Hold / Connection-passage / Read operations.

Decision:

- Open.

# Boundary -> Bearing family

Generator:

- Boundary

Generated mechanic:

- Current pressure from Generation: localisation / located distinction.

Immediate inheritance:

- Availability appears immediately generated by Boundary as presentability of located distinction.
- Strain appears immediately generated by Availability as present distinction under contrast.
- Bearing appears immediately generated by Strain as sustained pressure.
- Resolution appears immediately generated by Bearing plus Clearance as determined held difference at the current scope.

Ancestral inheritance:

- Resolution may inherit Boundary ancestrally through Availability, Strain, and Bearing.
- Bearing may inherit Boundary ancestrally through Availability and Strain.

Dependencies:

- Boundary depends on Distinction.
- Availability depends on Boundary.
- Strain depends on Availability.
- Bearing depends on Strain.
- Resolution depends on Bearing and Clearance.

Determination pressure:

- Strong.
- Boundary -> Availability -> Strain -> Bearing -> Resolution currently reads as located distinction becoming presentable, pressured, sustained, then determined.
- Resolution is terminal at the current scope: the chain closes a first-order condition without ending wider relation.

Appears through:

- Availability
- Strain
- Bearing
- Resolution

Observed reads:

- Boundary locates distinction without separating relation absolutely.
- Availability makes located distinction presentable.
- Strain names available distinction under contrast.
- Bearing names strain held as readable condition.
- Resolution names bearing determined at the current scope of evaluation.
- Resolution requires Clearance as well as Bearing.

Pressure:

- Boundary -> Bearing family is too compressed if Boundary generates localisation and Bearing is several determinations downstream.
- Strain, Bearing, and Resolution may be successive determinations of located distinction rather than siblings in one family.
- Resolution confirms terminal determination at current scope; it does not simply continue open bearing.
- Clearance appears at Resolution as an additional crossing condition into readable determination.

Must recover:

- Whether Boundary's generated mechanic should be named localisation, located distinction, or passage edge.
- Whether Strain, Bearing, and Resolution should be read as determination-depth stages rather than one inheritance family.
- Whether determination depth is a general read that appears in other primitive chains or only in the Boundary chain.
- Whether Clearance marks a crossing condition into determination rather than a direct Boundary inheritance.

Decision:

- Open.
- Boundary -> Bearing remains too compressed.
- Current recovery supports Boundary -> localisation, with Availability / Strain / Bearing / Resolution as determination-depth pressure.

# Availability -> Participation family

Generator:

- Availability

Generated mechanic:

- Current pressure from Generation: presentability / enterability.

Immediate inheritance:

- Presence may be immediate local availability of Coupling.
- Participation appears immediately generated by Carrying + Presence + Bearing Relation entering active scope.

Ancestral inheritance:

- Participation may inherit Availability ancestrally through Presence.

Dependencies:

- Presence depends on Coupling.
- Bearing Relation depends on Coupling, Presence, and Bearing.
- Participation depends on Carrying, Presence, and Bearing Relation.

Determination pressure:

- Moderate.
- Availability -> Presence -> Bearing Relation -> Participation may be availability becoming local, bearing, then active.
- This pressure is not yet clean because Availability is not an immediate dependency of Presence in current structure.

Appears through:

- Presence
- Bearing Relation
- Participation
- Participant
- Member

Observed reads:

- Availability names the condition through which located distinction can present.
- Presence names coupled relation locally available to read.
- Bearing Relation names coupled relation bearing on coupled relation.
- Participation names a bearing relation actively taking part in a scope.

Pressure:

- Participation is not simply Place-family.
- Availability -> Participation is too compressed if Availability generates presentability and Participation activates a present bearing relation.
- Participant and Member should not be tested until Participation is better placed.

Must recover:

- What from Availability remains recognisable through Presence, Bearing Relation, Participation, Participant, and Member.
- Whether Availability should be treated as ancestral rather than immediate in Participation.
- Whether Presence localises Availability or only uses availability language to read Coupling.

Decision:

- Open.

# Connection -> Coupling family

Generator:

- Connection

Generated mechanic:

- Current pressure from Generation: passage / held passage / directional availability.

Immediate inheritance:

- Carry and Trace appear immediately generated by Connection as directional availability.
- Carrying appears as second-order enactment of held forward passage.
- Recarry appears as returned or altered carrying through contact, trace, release, groundedness, and compatibility.
- Coupling appears immediately generated by Resolved Asymmetry + Carrying rather than by Connection.

Ancestral inheritance:

- Carrying inherits Connection ancestrally through Hold and Carry.
- Recarry inherits Connection ancestrally through Carrying and Trace.
- Coupling may inherit Connection ancestrally through Carrying and resolved relation.
- Nesting may inherit through Coupling rather than directly from Connection.

Dependencies:

- Connection depends on Relation.
- Carry / Trace depend on Connection and Hold.
- Carrying depends on Hold and Carry, and traces Connection.
- Recarry depends on Carrying, Trace, Contact, Groundedness, Release, and Compatibility.
- Coupling depends on Resolved Asymmetry and Carrying.

Determination pressure:

- Strong, but different from Boundary.
- Connection -> Carry/Trace -> Carrying -> Recarry reads as passage becoming directional, enacted, then returned through altered contact.
- This is continuing-path depth, not terminal closure-depth.

Appears through:

- Carry
- Trace
- Carrying
- Recarry
- Coupling
- Couple
- Nest
- Nesting

Observed reads:

- Carry and Trace are two directions through one connection.
- Carrying is movement through held forward availability.
- Recarry is continuity preserved through altered contact and compatible wider grounding.
- Coupling holds distinct resolved conditions in mutual availability without collapse.
- Couple:Nest and Coupling:Nesting may not name the same ratio.
- Noun forms may mark gathered positions while -ing forms mark proceeding operations.

Pressure:

- Connection -> Coupling family is too compressed if Connection generates passage and Coupling generates mutual availability.
- Nesting may inherit Connection ancestrally but Coupling immediately.
- Determination pressure here does not terminate at Recarry; it remains continuing, altered, and available for further carrying.

Must recover:

- Whether Coupling and Nesting inherit one connection mechanic, or whether Nesting marks a later mechanic generated by Coupling.
- Whether the Connection family should be re-read as passage rather than coupling.
- Whether continuing-path depth should be named separately from terminal determination depth.

Decision:

- Open.
- Current recovery supports Connection -> Carry/Trace as passage, Carrying -> enacted passage, Recarry -> returned/altered passage, and Coupling -> mutual availability.

## Guard

Do not make a family canonical because it reads well.

Accept an inheritance only where the Atlas repeatedly recovers the same mechanic across the family.

If a member fails the read, decide whether the family is wrong, the generator is wrong, the dependency is being confused for inheritance, determination pressure is being mistaken for inheritance, or the note has drifted.

The Atlas is the test. The working read yields.
