# Ground

Ground names what Reality Mechanics acknowledges but does not derive. It is the source-limit the Atlas remains answerable to.

[[Relation]] is the primitive where the dependency spine begins. Ground names what holds prior to that beginning without being traced to. Ground holds [[Seed]], and Seed holds Relation before the dependency spine begins, without making Relation produced by a prior term. Ground is not a term inside the dependency spine. It is the source-limit the first-order boundary protects: the acknowledged-prior that keeps Relation primitive within the system without claiming to be the origin of everything. Ground is the [[Terminal]] below — the read-stop where digging downward ends while what is acknowledged continues underived.

Ground prevents the system from becoming self-sealing. It gives Reality Mechanics a place to remain answerable without claiming to produce what answers it.

## Places

Ground places the acknowledged-prior of [[Reality Mechanics]]. It sits before the dependency spine and marks the lower source-limit and read-stop of the system.

## Holds

Ground is held by nothing within the system — acknowledged but not derived. From that position it holds the first distinction between what is acknowledged and what is derived, and holds [[Seed]] as the Ground-level relation-to-Ground through which [[Relation]] is held before traceable order begins.

## Pairs

Ground pairs with [[Relation]]: Ground is acknowledged but not derived; Relation is the primitive condition from which the Atlas begins tracing. Ground also pairs with [[Seed]]: Seed names Ground as relation-to-Ground becomes carryable within the loop — what the highest formation traces back to is the same source condition that held the first relation.

## Traces

Ground is prior to the system's first-order boundary. It is acknowledged but not defined within the system.

## Nests

Ground nests below [[Root Order]] and [[Natural Order]] as the source-limit that lets the Atlas participate in what it does not claim to originate.

## Reads

Ground becomes recognisable where the system participates in something prior rather than engineering itself from nothing — where its terms can be checked against what they did not generate.
