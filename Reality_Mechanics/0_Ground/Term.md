# Term

Term names a structurally placed interpretation. It is not identical to the word, sign, image, arrangement, action, or thought that carries it. Those are possible carriers; the term is the interpretation made locatable through its place, dependencies, openings, and carrying relation.

A word or sign becomes a term where meaning, dependency, and opening can travel together. Different carriers may hold the same term where that structural placement remains recoverable; the same word may carry different terms where its interpretation or dependency placement changes. A term serves where its conditions remain traceable. Terminality, termination, and final closure belong to later reads of threshold, resolution, or closure.

## Places

Term places a structurally placed interpretation: a readable point that can be carried by a word, sign, image, arrangement, action, or thought without being identical to that carrier.

## Holds

Term is held by [[Root Order]].

## Pairs

[[Label]] — Term names with dependency, opening, and carried relation travelling together; Label marks without carrying traceable conditions. Each requires the other to be locatable: the distinction between carrying and marking can only hold where both conditions are present as possibilities at the ground level.

## Nests

Term nests wherever an interpretation can be placed, traced, read, and carried at scope.

## Reads

Term becomes recognisable where an interpretation remains locatable through its dependencies across one or more carriers, so meaning, dependency, opening, and carried relation travel together.

In the Atlas, the term conditions are Places, Holds, Pairs, Traces, Nests, Reads, and Carries. These conditions test whether an interpretation has become a term rather than only a word, sign, or label. A term without traceable conditions drifts back toward an ungrounded expression.

`Traces` records the dependency recoverable from the term toward what made it available. `Carries` records confirmed continuation into downstream terms that trace back to it. What the term might make possible is an opening, not yet a carry. Reality may already be carrying beyond the current read, but the Atlas does not convert prediction into dependency merely by writing it forward.

## Carries

Term carries [[Thread]], where a term, trace, and read remain followable through a readable length.

The specific conditions that govern closure at later orders — Scope, Closure Scope, Condition — are not carried by Term but traced from their respective structural positions.
