# Traversal

A read being followed through a closure scope.

Traversal is held by [[Closure Scope]].

## Places

Traversal places a read being followed through a closure scope.

## Holds

Traversal is held by [[Closure Scope]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Traversal is a singular condition through which closure scope is moved through. The lateral asymmetry appears in what traversal carries: [[Process]] ↔ [[Sequence]].

## Nests

Traversal nests where reads can be followed through available conditions within a bounded extent.

## Reads

Traversal becomes recognisable where reads can be followed through available conditions within a bounded extent — where movement through a scope is possible rather than merely presence within it.
