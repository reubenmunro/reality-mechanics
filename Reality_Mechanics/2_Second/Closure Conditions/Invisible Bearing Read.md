# Invisible Bearing Read

Invisible Bearing Read names bearing that carries participation without presenting as bearing. Where bearing names strain held as a readable condition, invisible bearing read names the condition through which that holding continues without being visible as holding. It is not hidden bearing — invisible bearing remains retraceable; what carries is not presented but can be recovered through retrace.

## Places

Invisible Bearing Read places bearing that carries participation without presenting as bearing.

## Holds

Invisible Bearing Read is held by [[Bearing]] and [[Invisible]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Invisible Bearing Read is a singular condition — a read of bearing through invisible conditions. The asymmetry is expressed in what it carries: [[Nesting Read]].

## Reads

Invisible Bearing Read becomes recognisable where carrying can be traced despite not presenting as carrying — where something holds without showing that it holds.
