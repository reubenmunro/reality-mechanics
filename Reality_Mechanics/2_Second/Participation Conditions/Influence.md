# Influence

One participating condition altering how another condition is read, carried, or made available.

Influence is held by [[Participation]], [[Effect]], and [[Readability]].

## Places

Influence places one participating condition altering how another condition is read, carried, or made available.

## Holds

Influence is held by [[Participation]], [[Effect]], and [[Readability]].

## Pairs

Influence is vertical. It names one participating condition altering how another is read, carried, or made available, and carries this forward into [[Enchantment]], [[Binding]], and [[Wonder]]. Influence holds [[Participation]], [[Effect]], and [[Readability]] — there is no lateral co-present condition that Influence requires and that requires Influence in return at this order.


## Traces

- [[Participation]]
- [[Effect]]
- [[Readability]]

## Nests

Influence nests where one participation changes the readability or continuation of another condition without becoming control by itself.

## Reads

Influence becomes recognisable where participation changes how another condition can be read, carried, constrained, opened, or continued.

## Carries


- [[Enchantment]]
- [[Binding]]
- [[Wonder]]
