# Bearing Relation

Coupled relation bearing on coupled relation.

## Places

Bearing Relation places coupled relation bearing on coupled relation.

## Holds

Bearing Relation is held by [[Coupling]], [[Presence]], and [[Bearing]]. Coupled relation must remain locally available and able to bear while active participation proceeds.

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Bearing Relation is a singular condition at its juncture — the named structural connection between coupled conditions. There is no lateral contrast required to locate bearing relation. The structural asymmetry is fully expressed in [[Participation]]: bearing relation does not finish before participation begins; participation is bearing relation remaining active while it takes part.

## Traces

- [[Coupling]]
- [[Presence]]
- [[Bearing]]

## Nests

Bearing Relation nests as a second-order bridge between coupling, presence, and participation. It is singular and structural, and should not be collapsed into the third-order branch family Bearing Relations.

## Reads

Bearing Relation becomes recognisable where coupled relation does not merely remain mutually available, but bears on what it is coupled with so that participation can become active.
