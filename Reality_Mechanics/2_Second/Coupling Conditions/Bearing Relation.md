# Bearing Relation

Bearing Relation names coupled relation bearing on coupled relation. Where coupling names distinct resolved conditions held in relation and presence makes that coupling locally available, bearing relation names those coupled conditions beginning to bear on each other within a scope.

## Places

Bearing Relation places coupled relation bearing on coupled relation.

## Holds

Bearing Relation is held by [[Coupling]], [[Presence]], and [[Bearing]]. Coupled relation must be locally available and able to bear before active participation can begin.

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Bearing Relation is a singular condition at its juncture — the named structural connection between coupled conditions. There is no lateral contrast required to locate bearing relation. The structural asymmetry is fully expressed by what bearing relation becomes: [[Participation]].

## Traces

- [[Coupling]]
- [[Presence]]
- [[Bearing]]

## Nests

Bearing Relation nests as a second-order bridge between coupling, presence, and participation. It is singular and structural, and should not be collapsed into the third-order branch family Bearing Relations.

## Reads

Bearing Relation becomes recognisable where coupled relation does not merely remain mutually available, but bears on what it is coupled with so that participation can become active.
