# Form

Form names readable arrangement. Where difference names distinguishable non-sameness, form names difference held as an arrangement — multiple distinctions readable together as one organised condition. Physical shape can be a form, but form is not limited to shape; it names any arrangement of differences that can be read as one thing.

## Places

Form places readable arrangement.

## Holds

Form is held by [[Difference]] and [[Closure Scope]].

## Pairs

Form pairs with [[Information]]: form names the shape of difference at a closure scope — how the difference is configured, what contour it takes; information names the content of that same difference — what that configuration carries and makes readable. Both hold [[Difference]] and [[Closure Scope]] and neither grounds the other. Each requires the other to be locatable: form without information is empty structure; information without form is unplaceable content.

## Traces

- [[Difference]]
- [[Closure Scope]]

## Nests

Form nests where differences are held as one readable arrangement rather than isolated non-sameness.

## Reads

Form becomes recognisable where difference is held as arrangement — where multiple distinctions can be read together rather than as isolated non-sameness.
