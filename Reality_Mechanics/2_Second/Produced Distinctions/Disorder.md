# Disorder

Stability no longer holding.

Disorder is held by [[Stability]] and [[Strain]].

Disorder is shaded order, not a positive thing beside order. It is order under loss, occlusion, interruption, residue, or unreadability at the current scope.

## Places

Disorder places stability no longer holding.

## Holds

Disorder is held by [[Stability]] and [[Strain]].

## Pairs

Disorder pairs with [[Stability]]. Stability names identity and coherence holding; Disorder names that holding becoming unreadable.

## Traces

- [[Stability]]
- [[Strain]]

## Nests

Disorder nests where identity, coherence, or compatibility no longer holds within the current scope.

## Reads

Disorder becomes recognisable where a previously stable condition can no longer be read as holding — where identity, coherence, or compatibility has broken down within the current scope.
