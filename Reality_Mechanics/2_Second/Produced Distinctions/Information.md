# Information

Difference that makes a read available.

## Places

Information places difference that makes a read available.

## Holds

Information is held by [[Difference]] and [[Closure Scope]].

## Pairs

Information pairs with [[Form]]: information names the content of difference at a closure scope — what the difference carries and makes readable; form names the shape of that same difference — how it is configured and what contour it takes. Both hold [[Difference]] and [[Closure Scope]] and neither grounds the other. Each requires the other to be locatable: information without form is unplaceable; form without information is empty structure.

## Traces

- [[Difference]]
- [[Closure Scope]]
- [[Scale]]

## Nests

Information nests where difference changes what becomes available to evaluation.

## Reads

Information becomes recognisable where a difference can be read as changing what is available to evaluation — where not-same produces a readable alteration rather than mere distinction.

## Carries


- [[Know]]
