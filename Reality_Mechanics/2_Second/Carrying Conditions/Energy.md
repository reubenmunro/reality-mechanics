# Energy

Energy names generated availability for carrying.

This does not replace physical, biological, or domain-specific definitions of energy. It places what energy does in the Atlas order: energy names availability generated within a condition that can carry work, movement, transformation, maintenance, attention, repair, or continuation.

The core read is:

```text
Energy is generated availability for carrying.
```

## Places

Energy places generated availability for carrying.

## Holds

Energy is held by [[Generic]], [[Carrying]], [[Capacity]], [[Pressure]], and [[Flow]].

Generic names the generative source condition. Carrying names held forward availability being enacted. Capacity names supportable carrying. Pressure names load becoming locally borne through relation. Flow names carrying continuing through a medium.

Energy becomes readable where generation makes carrying available as capacity, pressure, flow, work, movement, transformation, maintenance, attention, repair, or continuation.

## Pairs

No universal lateral pair is required at this placement yet. Energy names generated availability for carrying; its local oppositions appear as depletion, dissipation, overload, or incompatible release in particular domains.

## Nests

Energy nests inside carrying as generated availability that can support work, movement, transformation, maintenance, attention, repair, or continuation.

It is not the source itself. It is the availability generated from a source or condition and made readable by what it can carry.

## Reads

Energy becomes recognisable where generation produces carrying availability.

The structural movement is:

```text
generation
-> available carrying
-> capacity, pressure, flow
-> work, movement, transformation, maintenance, repair, or continuation
```

Physical energy can be read where generated availability carries work, movement, heat, field relation, or transformation. Living energy can be read where metabolism converts intake, rest, strain, and repair into continued participation. Human energy can be read where received support, relation, nourishment, orientation, rest, or purpose becomes capacity to carry attention, care, work, speech, or action.

Energy becomes disorderly where availability continues without trace to what generated it, where it is extracted beyond compatible carrying, or where it is retained, released, or accelerated beyond the carrier's resolution band.
