# Compatibility

The condition through which what is being carried can continue through the conditions it moves into.

Compatibility is held by [[Carrying]] and [[Coupling]].

## Places

Compatibility places the condition through which what is being carried can continue through the conditions it moves into.

## Holds

Compatibility is held by [[Carrying]] and [[Coupling]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Compatibility is a singular condition — the mutual availability of carried conditions through coupling. The asymmetry is expressed in what compatibility carries: [[Stability]], [[Capacity]], and [[Know]].

## Traces

- [[Carrying]]
- [[Coupling]]

## Nests

Compatibility nests as a passage condition for carrying. It regulates whether carrying can continue through encountered conditions.

## Reads

Compatibility becomes recognisable where carrying can be evaluated as supportable — where what is being carried and the conditions it moves through can be read as holding together at the current scope.
