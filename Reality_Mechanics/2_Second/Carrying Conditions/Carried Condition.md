# Carried Condition

Carried Condition names the carrying condition returned to when load has concentrated too tightly in the participant or carrier.

The root sentence is:

```text
You are carried.
```

This is not reassurance added to carrying. It names a mechanics of carrying: before a participant carries, carrying is already occurring. Ground, gravity, weight, breath, support, and interval can take part of the load before meaning, interpretation, or decision returns.

Where load exceeds solitary carrying, meaning can wait.

## Places

Carried Condition places the carrying condition returned to when load has concentrated too tightly in the participant or carrier.

## Holds

Carried Condition is held by [[Carrying]], [[Ground]], [[Load]], [[Capacity]], and [[Overload]].

Carrying names the movement being restored. Ground names what is acknowledged before the system derives its own terms. Load and Capacity name what is being borne and what can support it. Overload names the excess condition that makes return to prior carrying conditions necessary.

## Pairs

Carried Condition pairs with [[Overload]]. Overload names load exceeding supportable capacity; Carried Condition names return to prior carrying conditions before interpretation or further carrying.

## Nests

Carried Condition nests inside [[Carrying]] as a return condition: where the carrier stops treating itself as the only carrier and lets prior carrying conditions take load.

It is not only an emergency condition. It becomes readable in fatigue, burnout, illness, shock, collapse, grief, accident, disorder, recovery, and ordinary overload wherever self-carrying has narrowed too much.

## Reads

Carried Condition becomes recognisable where load, pressure, fatigue, shock, illness, burnout, collapse, or ordinary overload exceeds self-carrying and the first movement is return to carried condition.

The movement is:

```text
load concentrates
-> self-carrying narrows
-> return to carried condition
-> ground and gravity take load
-> breath gives interval
-> carrying becomes available again
```

In practice this can read as:

```text
You are carried.
Lower to ground if possible.
Let the ground hold weight.
Feel gravity and ground together.
Stay with one breath, then one more.
Meaning can wait.
```

This does not replace care, emergency support, medical care, or direct human response where those are needed. It places the mechanical first return: carrying conditions before interpretation.
