# Medium

Medium names what carrying occurs through. Where carrying names held and placeable forward availability being enacted, medium names the condition that carrying moves through. Substance can be a medium, but medium first names the through-condition of carrying.

A medium allows some reads while thinning or blocking others. A phone call may allow voice, tone, and timing while not allowing enough behaviour read for some relational repair.

## Places

Medium places what carrying occurs through.

## Holds

Medium is held by [[Carrying]], [[Participation]], and [[Allow]].

## Pairs

Medium is vertical. It names what carrying occurs through, and carries this forward into [[Structure]], [[Flow]], and [[Current]]. Medium holds [[Carrying]], [[Participation]], and [[Allow]] — the passage condition of allowance makes medium available. There is no lateral co-present condition required for Medium to be locatable at this order.


## Traces

- [[Carrying]]
- [[Participation]]

## Nests

Medium nests as the through-condition of carrying and participation. It shapes what can be allowed through.

## Reads

Medium becomes recognisable where carrying can be read as occurring through something — where participation passes through a condition rather than independently of it, and where that condition shapes what can be allowed through.
