# Integration

Intake resolved into compatible carrying without erasing the distinction of what entered.

Integration is held by [[Intake]], [[Resolution]], [[Regulation]], and [[Compatibility]]. What enters must be available within carrying, resolved enough to participate, maintainable within the receiving condition, and compatible with what carries it before integration can be named.

[[Intake]] names availability crossing inward through a boundary. Integration names what happens where that intake becomes carried by the receiving order: it can participate in the condition, affect it, and be affected by it without remaining unresolved foreignness or being erased into sameness.

## Places

## Holds

Integration is held by [[Intake]], [[Resolution]], [[Regulation]], and [[Compatibility]]. What enters must be available within carrying, resolved enough to participate, maintainable within the receiving condition, and compatible with what carries it before integration can be named.

## Pairs

Integration pairs with [[Splinter]]. Integration names what has crossed inward and becomes carried by the receiving order; Splinter names what has crossed inward but remains unresolved within it. Each requires the other to be locatable: integration is readable against the possibility that intake remains foreign to the carrying order, while splinter is readable against the possibility that what entered could become compatibly carried.

## Nests

Integration nests inside carrying where what has entered becomes held by the receiving order without being erased or remaining unresolved foreignness.

## Reads

Integration becomes recognisable where what has entered can participate in the carrying condition, affect it, and be affected by it while remaining compatible with the order that now carries it.

Integration is not absorption into sameness. The entering distinction remains traceable even as it becomes part of wider carrying.
