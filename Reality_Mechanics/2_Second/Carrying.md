# Carrying

Carrying is not yet [[Retracing]]. [[Carry]] is the forward passage made available where relation is held. Carrying is movement through that passage. What carries may be resolved, strained, changing, or still being formed; [[Resolution]] can shape carrying without being a universal prerequisite for it.

Carrying does not create a separate relation from trace. The same connection that carries forward offers [[Trace]] backward. [[Retracing]] is a participant following that trace.

## Places

Carrying places held forward availability being enacted.

## Holds

Carrying is held by [[Hold]] and [[Carry]]. Forward passage must be held before it can be enacted.

## Pairs

Carrying pairs with [[Retracing]]. Carrying is movement through the forward availability of a held connection; Retracing is movement through the backward availability of that same connection.

## Traces

- [[Hold]]
- [[Carry]]
- [[Connection]]
- [[First Order Crossing|Threshold (First → Second)]]

## Nests

Carrying nests as enacted forward movement through connection. Resolution, transfer, holding, participation, and other conditions may shape carrying without defining every instance of it.

## Reads

Carrying becomes recognisable where held forward availability is being entered or continued, whether what passes is resolved, strained, changing, or still being formed.
