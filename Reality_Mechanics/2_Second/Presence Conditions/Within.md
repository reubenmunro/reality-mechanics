# Within

Within names relation held inside a cleared boundary. Where in carries interior placement and clearance opens the relational space that allows alongside-ness, within names their compound: relation that is both inside and available within a bounded condition. Enclosure can show within, but within names interior placement under available clearance.

## Places

Within places relation held inside a cleared boundary.

## Holds

Within is held by [[In]], [[Clearance]], and [[Boundary]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Within is a singular condition — the condition of being inside a clearance and boundary. The asymmetry is expressed in what within carries: [[Nested Relation]].

## Traces

- [[In]]
- [[Clearance]]
- [[Boundary]]

## Nests

Within nests as the compound of interior placement and available clearance: relation held inside a bounded relational space.

## Reads

Within becomes recognisable where relation can be read as inside and alongside a bounded condition — where something participates from within rather than from outside or alongside only.
