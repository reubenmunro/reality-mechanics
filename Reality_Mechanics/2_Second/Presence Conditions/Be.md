# Be

Presence holding.

Be is held by [[Presence]].

## Places

Be places presence holding.

## Holds

Be is held by [[Presence]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Be is a singular condition — the minimal presence required for anything to be at all. There is no lateral contrast at the be level. The asymmetry is expressed in what be becomes: [[Being]], [[Exist]], and [[Become]].

## Traces

- [[Presence]]

## Nests

Be nests under presence as presence holding without requiring permanence.

## Reads

Be becomes recognisable where presence can be read as continuing rather than merely having appeared — where something is present now rather than only once.
