# Coupled Asymmetry

Resolved asymmetry held between coupled conditions.

## Places

Coupled Asymmetry places resolved asymmetry held between coupled conditions.

## Holds

Coupled Asymmetry is held by [[Resolved Asymmetry]] and [[Coupling]]. Asymmetry must be determined, and conditions must be held in relation, before unevenness can persist through coupling.

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Coupled Asymmetry is a singular condition — resolved asymmetry held together through coupling. There is no lateral contrast required at this level.

## Traces

- [[Resolved Asymmetry]]
- [[Coupling]]

## Nests

Coupled Asymmetry nests where asymmetry persists between coupled conditions without collapsing the coupling into sameness.

## Reads

Coupled asymmetry becomes recognisable where two coupled conditions can be read as uneven — where the coupling holds difference rather than resolving it into sameness.
