# Presence

Coupled relation locally available to read.

Presence is held by [[Coupling]]. Coupled relation remains held while it is locally available within scope; coupling does not finish before presence begins.

## Places

Presence places coupled relation locally available to read.

## Holds

Presence is held by [[Coupling]]. Coupled relation remains held while it is locally available within scope; coupling does not finish before presence begins.

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Presence is a singular condition at its juncture — the condition under which coupling becomes available for participation. There is no lateral contrast required to locate presence. The structural asymmetry is fully expressed in [[Bearing Relation]]: presence does not finish before bearing relation begins; bearing relation is presence remaining available while it bears on what it is coupled with.

## Nests

Presence nests as a second-order root condition. It makes coupled relation encounterable without reducing presence to appearance.

## Reads

Presence becomes recognisable where relation is available as this rather than not-this, so something can be encountered within the current scope rather than only implied.
