# Threshold (Second → Third)

This threshold names the crossing from Second Order into Third Order.

## Places

Threshold (Second → Third) places the crossing where carrying through recurring participation begins to organise into a field.

## Holds

Threshold (Second → Third) is held by [[Second Order]], [[Participation]], and [[Recurrence]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward — marks the transition into [[Field of Participation]] and [[Third Order]]

## Traces

- [[Second Order]]
- [[Participation]]
- [[Recurrence]]

## Nests

Threshold (Second → Third) nests between orders. It marks a specific passage, not a general threshold.

## Reads

Threshold (Second → Third) becomes recognisable where carrying through participation recurs enough to organise into a field of participation.

**What Second Order makes available.**

Carrying enacts held and placeable forward availability. Participation names carrying actively taking part in a scope through presence and bearing relation.

**The crossing.**

Where participation recurs, it begins to organise into recognisable fields. The first third-order condition is Field of Participation — recurring organisation arising through participation.
