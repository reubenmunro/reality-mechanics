# Reality-Bearing Connection

A connection that can carry reality through trace, read, bearing, and retrace.

Reality-Bearing Connection is held by [[Atlas]], [[Bearing]], and [[Retrace Read|Retrace]].

A reality-bearing connection keeps relation readable, traceable, and answerable across fields without flattening the fields into each other.

Reality-bearing relation is coupled enough to participate, and nested enough to be ordered. Coupling gives connection; nesting gives dependency order. Connection without nesting becomes flat; nesting without coupling becomes detached. A reality-bearing connection requires both: connection enough to participate, and asymmetry enough to remain traceable.

## Places

Reality-Bearing Connection places a connection that can carry reality through trace, read, bearing, and retrace.

## Holds

Reality-Bearing Connection is held by [[Atlas]], [[Bearing]], and [[Retrace Read|Retrace]].

## Pairs

Reality-Bearing Connection is vertical. It names a connection that can carry reality through trace, read, bearing, and retrace, and carries this forward into [[Atlasing]]. Reality-Bearing Connection holds [[Atlas]], [[Bearing]], and [[Retrace Read]] — it is the upstream condition that makes Atlasing available. There is no lateral co-present condition that Reality-Bearing Connection requires and that requires it in return at this order.

## Nests

Reality-Bearing Connection nests where notes, terms, supports, reads, and retraces carry reality rather than mere information.

## Reads

Reality-Bearing Connection becomes recognisable where a link, placement, or relation is coupled enough to participate and nested enough to be ordered, so it can be followed back through what supports it and still carry the reality it claims to connect.
