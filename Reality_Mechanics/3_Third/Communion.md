# Communion

Communion names the invariant condition of genuine collective participation: distinct participations genuinely together without collapsing into sameness.

[[Organic Field]] names participation organising itself through natural order. Communion is the prior condition organic field requires: the participation must be genuine before it can organise into a field.

[[Organised Domain]] names intentionally arranged participation. Communion is the prior condition organised arrangement requires: the participation must be genuine before arrangement can carry it rather than merely simulate it.

Communion is not aggregation. Aggregation places many together without genuine participation passing between them. Communion holds the gathering while genuine participation remains — many-while-genuinely-together is the structural signature of Communion.

Communion is hidden in functioning fields and domains because it is not the visible operation. It becomes clearest in absence: where form persists while genuine togetherness has gone, Communion was not present.

## Places

Communion places the invariant condition of genuine collective participation before the split into organic field and organised domain.

## Holds

Communion is held by [[Field of Participation]]. A field of participation must be established before distinct participations can be genuinely together.

## Pairs

Field of Participation carries downward — into Communion. Communion is an invariant carrier: it holds whether or not the split into organic or organised is visible.

## Nests

Communion nests before the organic/organised split. It lets organic fields arise through genuine participation and organised domains carry genuine participation into arrangement rather than merely simulate it.

## Reads

Communion becomes recognisable where distinct participations are genuinely together: not merely co-present, coordinated, or aggregated, but held in a participation that something passes through.

It is also recognisable in absence, where members are present and procedures continue but nothing genuine passes between them.
