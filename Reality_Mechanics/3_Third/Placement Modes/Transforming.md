# Transforming

Transforming names the placement mode where participation is primarily carried through change of form, function, condition, or arrangement.

## Places

Transforming places the mode where participation is primarily carried through change of form, function, condition, or arrangement — where what is carried becomes otherwise while remaining traceable through what changed.

## Holds

Transforming is held by [[Placement Mode]], [[Form]], [[Function]], and [[Carrying]]. A mode of participation must exist, form and function must be present, and carrying must persist through the change before alteration can be placed as the primary mode.

## Pairs

Transforming pairs with [[Sustaining]]. Transforming names carrying that changes form; Sustaining names carrying that continues without change of form.

## Traces

- [[Placement Mode]]
- [[Form]]
- [[Function]]
- [[Carrying]]

## Nests

Transforming nests within Placement Modes as the change mode — where the primary movement is a carried condition becoming otherwise while its trace remains followable.

## Reads

Transforming becomes recognisable where a note is primarily placed by a carried condition becoming otherwise while remaining traceable through what changed.

## Carries

This note carries no further public branch at this scope.
