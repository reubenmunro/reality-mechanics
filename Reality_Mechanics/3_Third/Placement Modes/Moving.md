# Moving

Moving names the placement mode where participation is primarily carried through traversal, change of position, action, or directional proceeding.

## Places

Moving places the mode where participation is primarily carried through traversal, change of position, or directional proceeding — where what is carried moves through a route rather than staying situated.

## Holds

Moving is held by [[Placement Mode]], [[Traversal]], [[Proceed]], and [[Carrying]]. A mode of participation must exist, traversal must be available, a direction of proceeding must be readable, and carrying must persist before change of position can be placed as the primary mode.

## Pairs

Moving pairs with [[Locating]]. Locating names where something is positioned; Moving names the traversal through which that position changes.

## Traces

- [[Placement Mode]]
- [[Traversal]]
- [[Proceed]]
- [[Carrying]]

## Nests

Moving nests within Placement Modes as the traversal mode — where the primary movement is participation changing location, path, or action through a carried route.

## Reads

Moving becomes recognisable where a note is primarily placed by participation changing location, state, path, posture, or action through a carried route.
