# Expressing

Expressing names the placement mode where participation is primarily carried through outward readable form.

## Places

Expressing places the mode where participation is primarily carried through outward readable form — where relation becomes available to others through gesture, language, sound, image, or movement.

## Holds

Expressing is held by [[Placement Mode]], [[Read]], [[Form]], and [[Medium]]. A mode of participation must exist, readability must be carriable outward, form must hold the expression, and a medium must transmit it before outward form can be placed as the primary mode.

## Pairs

Expressing pairs with [[Knowing]]. Knowing names the inward direction — reading in toward distinction and understanding; Expressing names the outward direction — making readable form available to others.

## Traces

- [[Placement Mode]]
- [[Read]]
- [[Form]]
- [[Medium]]

## Nests

Expressing nests within Placement Modes as the outward mode — where the primary movement is relation becoming visible, hearable, or readable through form.

## Reads

Expressing becomes recognisable where a note is primarily placed by relation becoming outwardly readable through gesture, language, feeling, sound, image, movement, or form.

This note carries no further public branch at this scope.
