# Sustaining

The mode where participation is primarily carried through continuation, maintenance, or recovery of carrying — where what matters is keeping a condition going without losing compatibility.

Sustaining is held by [[Placement Mode]], [[Carrying]], [[Recurrence]], and [[Compatibility]]. A mode of participation must exist, carrying must be ongoing, recurrence must show the condition holding over time, and compatibility must be preservable before continuation can be placed as the primary mode.

## Places

## Pairs

Sustaining pairs with [[Transforming]]. Sustaining names carrying that continues without change of form; Transforming names carrying that changes form while remaining traceable.

## Nests

Sustaining nests within Placement Modes as the continuation mode — where the primary movement is preserving, restoring, or maintaining carrying rather than changing, transmitting, or locating it.

## Reads

Sustaining becomes recognisable where a note is primarily placed by what keeps a condition going, restores it, preserves it, or lets it continue without losing compatibility.
