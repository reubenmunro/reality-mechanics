# Alive

The condition through which coupled carrying is sustained through recursive regulation — a living condition that persists by continuing to regulate its own carrying.

Alive is held by [[Life]], [[Live]], [[Coupling]], [[Carrying]], [[Recursive Regulation]], [[Compatibility]], and [[Adaptation]]. Coupling must hold, recursive regulation must sustain compatible carrying, and adaptation must remain possible before the condition can be named as alive.

This note places [[Live]] inside [[Life]] as living continuation rather than general liveness.

## Reads

Alive becomes readable where living coupling can still maintain, repair, adapt, respond, resume, or continue compatible carrying at the evaluated scope.

Alive depends on adaptation in this structural sense: local resolutions may complete, but the living condition can still re-enter, reorient, repair, or continue without treating the larger asymmetry as finished.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Bearing]] holds available relation.

Second: [[Live]], [[Coupling]], [[Carrying]], [[Recursive Regulation]], and [[Compatibility]] make living continuation evaluable at a [[Closure Scope]].

Third: [[Alive]] places that structural read inside [[Life]].

Distinction:

A live condition may not be alive.

An alive condition must remain live somewhere, but may not be outwardly active at every scope.
