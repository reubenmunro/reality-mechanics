# Our Story

Our Story names the organic field where participation becomes readable through inherited, remembered, narrated, and retraced continuity.

## Places

Our Story places the third-order field where participation carries prior relation forward through memory, inheritance, account, and retrace — the structural field for shared relational history.

## Holds

Our Story is held by [[Participation]] and [[Field of Participation]]. Participation must structure a field and prior carrying must be available to retrace before shared relational history can be named.

## Pairs

Our Story pairs with [[Natural World]]. Our Story names the field of shared relational history carried through participation; Natural World names the physical field within which that history is grounded. Each requires the other: history is always carried within a world; the world becomes readable through the history of participation within it.
