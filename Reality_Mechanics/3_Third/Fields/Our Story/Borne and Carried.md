# Borne and Carried

Borne and Carried names the human-scope read that carrying becomes possible because the human first arrives borne and carried.

The core sentence is:

```text
We can carry because we were borne and carried.
```

This is not sentiment added to the structure. It names a received condition of human participation. Human carrying does not begin as self-originating capacity. The human arrives already carried by body, relation, language, care, world, ancestry, field, and circumstance.

[[Carried Condition]] names the more basic carrying mechanics under this human-scope read: a participant can return to carried condition before meaning, interpretation, or further carrying has to resume.

## Places

Borne and Carried places the human-scope read that carrying becomes possible because the human first arrives borne and carried.

## Holds

Borne and Carried is held by [[Life]], [[Our Story Field]], [[Carrying]], [[Carried Condition]], [[Inheritance]], and [[Responsibility]].

Living arrival gives the condition its life bearing. Our Story Field places the shared and inherited field. Carrying names what becomes possible. Carried Condition names return to prior carrying where load has concentrated too tightly. Inheritance names received prior carrying. Responsibility names the answerability that emerges where received carrying becomes active participation.

## Pairs

Borne and Carried pairs with [[Carrying Better]]. Borne and Carried names the received origin of human carrying; Carrying Better names the correction and improvement of what has been received.

## Nests

Borne and Carried nests within the [[Our Story Field]] as a human carrying read: received carrying becoming the capacity and obligation to carry.

It is human-scope, not ground-scope. Carrying can be read structurally across orders, but this note places the human reason for carrying inside the field where human history, memory, inheritance, care, and responsibility become readable.

## Reads

Borne and Carried becomes recognisable where human carrying is not self-originating but begins from being borne by prior carrying.

The movement is:

```text
borne
-> carried
-> able to carry
-> answerable for carrying
-> capable of regeneration
```

Responsibility does not appear here as imposed debt. It appears because received carrying becomes answerable carrying. A participant who has been carried into the capacity to carry can become responsible for what their carrying affects.

Regeneration also changes under this read. Regeneration is not self-repair by will alone. It is return to the conditions that made carrying possible, so carrying can become available again.
