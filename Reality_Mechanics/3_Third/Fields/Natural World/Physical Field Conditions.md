Shared Read:

Physical space, light, gravity, black holes, Casimir plates, neutrinos, dark matter, dark energy, and cosmic polarity test [[Field Availability Pattern]].

[[Field Bearing]] marks the point where altered field availability becomes bearing on traversal, escape, attraction, closure, visibility, or measurable path.

In physical space, located clearance becomes readable as measurable extent, distance, position, or traversable field relation.

In a black hole, gravitational collapse makes an event boundary decisive for escape and outside read.

In Casimir plates, arranged material boundaries alter available field modes enough for constrained field availability to bear on the plates as a measurable force.

In neutrinos, traversal remains broadly available while local visibility depends on rare coupling and indirect detection.

In light, clearance becomes readable as apparent traversal, separation, visibility, or field-event readability.

In gravity, located clearance becomes constrained so field bearing on traversal, fall, orbit, escape, and measurable path becomes physically readable.

In dark matter, [[Invisible Bearing]] becomes physically readable where gravitational [[Field Bearing]] remains readable while ordinary electromagnetic visibility remains unavailable.

In dark energy, large-scale cosmic clearance remains readable as expansion beyond ordinary gravitational closure, with [[Gravity]] supplying the ordinary closure-bearing condition read against.
