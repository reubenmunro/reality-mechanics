# Field Availability Pattern

The structural pattern through which field-bearing conditions make availability readable — the recurrent structural shape of how fields present their carrying to readability.

Field Availability Pattern names that availability changes. [[Field Bearing]] names where changed field availability bears on traversal, escape, attraction, closure, visibility, or measurable path.

## Places

Field Availability Pattern places the structural pattern through which field-bearing conditions make availability readable — the recurrent structural shape of how fields present their carrying to readability.

## Holds

Field Availability Pattern is held by [[Physical Field Conditions]], [[Pattern]], [[Field Bearing]], [[Invisible Bearing]], and [[Natural World]]. Physical field conditions must hold, bearing must be present in the field, and pattern must gather before the structural shape of field availability can be named.

Derivation:

[[Relation Read]] names relation read as joined spread under a shared condition.

[[Availability]] names what becomes present enough to participate in evaluation.

[[Pattern]] names recurrence made readable through compatible closure.

Field Availability Pattern names recurring reads of availability changing within field conditions.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Relation Read]] and [[Availability]] make altered physical-field availability readable.

Second: [[Field Bearing]], [[Visibility]], [[Invisible Bearing]], and [[Closure Scope]] make the alteration locally evaluable.

Third: [[Field Availability Pattern]] places that structural read inside natural-world physical-field conditions.

Pattern Forms:

- Apparent clearance: [[Light]]
- Constrained clearance: [[Gravity]]
- Boundary-limited availability: [[Black Hole]]
- Arranged-boundary availability: [[Casimir Plates]]
- Low-interaction traversal: [[Neutrino]]
- Visibility-limited bearing / invisible bearing: [[Dark Matter]]
- Expansion-scale clearance: [[Dark Energy]]
- Indirect detection: currently visible through [[Neutrino]], [[Dark Matter]], and [[Dark Energy]]
- Scale-dependent visibility: currently visible across [[Light]], [[Gravity]], [[Black Hole]], [[Casimir Plates]], [[Neutrino]], [[Dark Matter]], and [[Dark Energy]]

Pattern Movement:

```text
field condition
-> availability changes
-> changed availability bears on boundary, constraint, traversal, coupling, visibility, measurement, or scale
-> read becomes apparent, indirect, measurable, limited, or scale-bound
```
