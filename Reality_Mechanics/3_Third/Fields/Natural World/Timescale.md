# Timescale

The condition through which carrying is evaluable across different orders of temporal extent and resolution capacity — the structural read of how carrying, life, and natural conditions become readable at different scales.

Timescale is held by [[Time]], [[Readability]], [[Scale]], [[Rate]], [[Carrying]], [[Body]], [[Life Field]], and [[Natural World Field]]. Time must remain available as interval and continuation, while carrying, scale, rate, and resolution capacity make that continuation locally readable.

Different organisms, bodies, cells, and ecosystems are different local readings of held clearance, not different kinds of time. Each has a native resolution capacity: a rate and scale at which order can be distinguished, integrated, carried, and responded to.

Resolution is band-limited. A participant does not resolve all order; it resolves a band of order — the range of scale, rate, intensity, difference, and interval it can receive, integrate, and carry.

## Reads

Timescale becomes readable where held clearance is resolved locally enough for a participant, organism, cell, or ecosystem to carry relation at its own scale, rate, and native resolution band.

A being's apparent time is shaped by what it can resolve. Slow and fast are not only external measures; they are also local readings of how much order can be distinguished, integrated, and carried per interval. Learning, fatigue, injury, attention, and practice can therefore change the felt scale of time by changing how sequence is resolved.

What falls outside a participant's resolution band may still be real, effective, and traceable. It is not directly readable until it is slowed, amplified, translated, measured, inferred, or otherwise brought into a compatible carrying range.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Clearance]] and [[Time]] hold relation apart enough to remain readable.

Second: [[Scale]], [[Readability]], [[Rate]], and [[Carrying]] make local resolution evaluable under a carrying condition.

Third: [[Timescale]] places local readings of held clearance inside natural-world, body, and life participation.
