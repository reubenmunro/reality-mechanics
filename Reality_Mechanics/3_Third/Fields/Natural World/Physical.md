# Physical

Physical names natural-world readability where relation is met as material or field condition.

This note defines the physical component used by compounds such as [[Physical Space]] and [[Physical Field Conditions]].

## Places

Physical places the third-order field read where material and field conditions become readable as structural — where boundary, availability, and readability become evaluable in the natural world.

## Holds

Physical is held by [[Natural World Field]], [[Relation Read]], [[Boundary]], [[Availability]], [[Readability]], and [[Natural World]]. Boundary must locate material distinction and readability must be present before natural conditions can be named as physical field reads.

## Pairs

Physical pairs with [[Natural World]]. Physical names the material distinction condition; Natural World names the field in which physical conditions become readable through boundary, availability, and readability.

## Traces

- [[Natural World Field]]

## Nests

Physical nests within the Natural World Field as the field-level condition through which material and field relations become structurally readable.

## Reads

Physical becomes readable where natural-world relation can be sensed, located, measured, constrained, affected, or modelled as material or field condition.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Relation Read]], [[Boundary]], and [[Availability]] make natural-world condition readable as located relation.

Second: [[Readability]] makes that condition evaluable by sensing, measuring, locating, or modelling.

Third: [[Physical]] places that readability inside the [[Natural World Field]] as material or field condition.

## Carries

Physical carries [[Physical Field Conditions]] — the branch container that holds all specific physical field reads: Gravity, Field Availability Pattern, Physical Space, Timescale, Light, Neutrino, and Casimir Plates.
