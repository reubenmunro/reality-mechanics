# Natural World

The organic field where Natural Order becomes readable as physical, ecological, spatial, material, cosmic, and environmental participation.

## Places

Natural World places the organic field where Natural Order becomes readable as physical, ecological, spatial, material, cosmic, and environmental participation.

## Holds

Natural World is held by [[Natural Order]] and [[Field of Participation]].

## Pairs

Natural World pairs with [[Our Story]]. Natural World names the physical field within which participation is grounded — the world as material and ecological condition; Our Story names the field of shared relational history carried through participation within that world. Each requires the other: history is always carried within a world; the world becomes readable through the history of participation within it.

## Traces

- [[Natural Order]]
- [[Field of Participation]]

## Nests

Natural World nests as a fielded read of physical, organic, and participation-bearing world conditions.

## Reads

Natural World becomes recognisable where relation participates through physical, material, ecological, spatial, or cosmic organisation without requiring engineered arrangement.
