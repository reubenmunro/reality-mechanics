# Externalised Cognition

Externalised cognition names branch-level carrying where cognitive participation is distributed into external structure.

## Places

Externalised Cognition places the condition through which cognitive carrying is extended through a medium — thought, memory, or knowing carried beyond the participant's body through tool, record, or system.

## Holds

Externalised Cognition is held by [[Cognition]] and [[Medium]]. Cognition must be present and a medium must be able to carry the cognitive condition before externalised extension can be named.

## Pairs

No lateral pair is required at this placement yet; this term currently reads as a branch or terminal read.

## Traces

- [[Cognition]]
- [[Medium]]

## Nests

Externalised Cognition nests within the Cognition Field as the path read for cognitive carrying extended through medium — the structural ground for AI, documentation, and knowledge-system reads.

## Reads

Externalised Cognition becomes readable where cognitive participation is carried through an external medium or structure while remaining retraceable as cognition rather than becoming detached output.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Boundary]] and [[Availability]] must hold enough for cognitive participation to be located outside the body-mind loop.

Second: [[Medium]] and [[Carrying]] make that participation evaluable as externally supported cognition.

Third: [[Externalised Cognition]] places that carrying inside the [[Cognition Field]] as a cognition path read.
