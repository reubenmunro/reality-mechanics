# Place Field

The recurring field of participation through which location, fit, boundary, and belonging become readable as spatial, relational, and community position.

Place Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], and [[Placed Participation]]. [[Belong]] nests as a later read of compatible placed participation.

## Places

Place Field places a recurring field of participation.

## Holds

Place Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], and [[Placed Participation]]. [[Belong]] nests as a later read of compatible placed participation.

## Pairs

Place Field pairs with [[Society Field]]. Place Field names the field of located participation — where carrying is positioned and bounded by spatial and community fit; Society Field names the field of relational participation patterns among participants. Located participation is always organised by social patterns; social patterns are always carried somewhere.
