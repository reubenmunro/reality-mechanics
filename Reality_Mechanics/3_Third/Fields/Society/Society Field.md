# Society Field

The recurring field of participation through which patterns of consent, obligation, belonging, governance, care, conflict, and repair become readable between participants.

Society Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], and [[Society]]. [[Consent]] and [[Destination-Led Carrying]] nest as later reads; they do not hold the field that carries them.

## Places

Society Field places a recurring field of participation.

## Holds

Society Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], and [[Society]]. [[Consent]] and [[Destination-Led Carrying]] nest as later reads; they do not hold the field that carries them.

## Pairs

Society Field pairs with [[Place Field]]. Society Field names the field of recurring relational patterns among participants; Place Field names the field of located participation. Social patterns are always carried somewhere; places are only readable where patterns of participation recur within them.

## Reads

Society Field becomes recognisable where collective relation, consent, obligation, governance, conflict, care, or shared organisation makes participation social.

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Participation]], [[Boundary]], [[Consent]], [[Control]], [[Reciprocity]], and [[Governing]] can become terminal to this field as local reads such as [[Society]] and [[Consent]].

## Carries

Society Field carries [[Consent]], [[Civil]], and [[Law]] — the structural reads through which social patterns become evaluable through permission, public answerability, and formal direction. Nested reads are recorded under Nests; each traces its own structural ground.
