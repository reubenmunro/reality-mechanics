# Emotion

Emotion names patterned feeling that evaluates relation and pressures action, expression, care, protection, or repair.

Emotion grows from feeling where the read gathers pattern, direction, and action-pressure.

## Places

Emotion places the condition through which gathered pattern presses available bearing toward expression or action — the structural node where readability gathered from feeling presses on carrying.

## Holds

Emotion is held by [[Feeling]], [[Pattern]], [[Recognition Read|Recognition]], [[Strain]], [[Bearing]], and [[Compatibility]]. Feeling must make availability readable, pattern must gather, and strain must press bearing before the condition can be read as emotion.

Emotion pairs with [[Feeling]]. Feeling names the available detection of strain; Emotion names the condition where felt pattern gathers and presses bearing toward response.

Emotion becomes readable where feeling gathers enough pattern, recognition, and strain to pressure action, expression, protection, care, or repair.

First: [[Strain]] and [[Bearing]] allow felt pressure to be carried before action.

Second: [[Pattern]], [[Recognition Read|Recognition]], and [[Compatibility]] make emotional pressure readable and checkable.
