# Feeling

Feeling names embodied availability becoming readable to a participant.

A feeling is real as a read, but it does not decide cause, meaning, or action by itself. It shows that relation has become locally available.

## Places

Feeling places the condition through which the body makes change in availability detectable — the structural node where bodily sensing becomes a readable boundary condition.

## Holds

Feeling is held by [[Body]], [[Sensing]], [[Availability]], [[Boundary]], [[Strain]], and [[Readability]]. Bodily change must locate a detectable boundary before the availability of strain can be read as feeling.

## Pairs

Feeling pairs with [[Emotion]]. Feeling names available detection of bodily strain; Emotion names the condition where that detection gathers into pattern and presses bearing.

## Nests

Feeling nests within the Body Field as the condition through which availability becomes detectable — the structural ground for emotion, sensation, and affect reads.

## Reads

Feeling becomes readable where embodied change is sensed as locally available to the participant before it has been fully patterned, named, expressed, or acted on.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Availability]], [[Boundary]], and [[Strain]] make embodied change present enough to be carried.

Second: [[Readability]] makes that embodied availability available to read.

Third: [[Feeling]] places sensed availability inside [[Body Field]] with bridge pressure from mind, relation, and expression.

Movement:

```text
body detects change
-> availability becomes felt
-> feeling can be borne
-> meaning and action remain to be checked
```

## Carries

Feeling carries [[Emotion]]. Where feeling gathers enough pattern and recognition, available detection becomes patterned pressure on bearing.
