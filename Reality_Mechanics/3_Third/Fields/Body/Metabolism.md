## Reads

Metabolism becomes readable where a living system converts intake, strain, energy, rest, repair, waste, and activity into continued participation at a particular rate and pattern.

Different living systems resolve at different metabolic rates and patterns. A small bird, a tree, a human body, and a damaged body do not carry change in the same way. Metabolism names the rate and manner of resolution, not a simple measure of efficiency.

In humans, metabolism can include movement, thought, language, craft, emotion, relation, repair, rest, and care as pathways through which available strain and energy are resolved. The traceable question is whether the living carrier remains nourished, repairable, and compatibly coupled to what it is resolving.
