# Making

Making names the organic field where participation brings form, tool, work, or arrangement into availability.

## Places

Making places the third-order field where relation is carried into form through action, material, tool, method, or craft — the structural field for production, fabrication, and intentional forming.

## Holds

Making is held by [[Participation]] and [[Field of Participation]]. Participation must structure a field before relation carried into form can be named as making.

## Pairs

Making pairs with [[Tool]]. Making names the act of carrying relation into form; Tool names the produced form that making carries into further participation as an augmenting condition.

Making carries [[Tool]], [[Weapon]], and [[Hidden Carrying]] — Tool names capacity extended through made form; Weapon names capacity redirected toward force, harm, or override; Hidden Carrying names the displaced dependencies that produced form may obscure.
