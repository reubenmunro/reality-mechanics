# Hidden Carrying

The condition through which a made arrangement carries dependencies that are displaced, obscured, or unaccounted — carrying whose structural load is present but not surfaced.

Hidden Carrying is held by [[Carrying]], [[Making Field]], [[Invisible]], [[Dependency]], and [[Terms of Service]]. Carrying must proceed and a dependency must be present but unsurfaced before the displaced structural load can be named as hidden.

## Places

Hidden Carrying places the condition through which a made arrangement carries dependencies that are displaced, obscured, or unaccounted — carrying whose structural load is present but not surfaced.

## Holds

Hidden Carrying is held by [[Carrying]], [[Making Field]], [[Invisible]], [[Dependency]], and [[Terms of Service]]. Carrying must proceed and a dependency must be present but unsurfaced before the displaced structural load can be named as hidden.

## Pairs

Hidden Carrying pairs with [[Tool]]. Tool names the produced form that makes carrying available and visible; Hidden Carrying names the condition where produced form displaces and obscures the carrying it depends on. Each is a way making carries forward: Tool surfaces function; Hidden Carrying conceals dependency.

## Nests

Hidden Carrying nests within the Making Field as the condition through which produced arrangements displace their carrying dependencies — the structural ground for AI coupling and terms-of-service reads.

## Reads

Hidden Carrying becomes recognisable where a tool, system, or invention is described as clean, seamless, frictionless, endless, or dependency-free while the carrying relation has not disappeared, but moved.

Structural Test:

A grounded invention should make its carrying relations more readable: what energy, material, land, labour, waste, repair, risk, governance, or future consequence carries it, and who carries the cost if it fails.

Hidden Carrying belongs in the making field where produced arrangements need their displaced dependencies to remain traceable, and the making field must be entered for those displaced dependencies to become readable as carrying rather than as absence of carrying.
