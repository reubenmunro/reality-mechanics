# Making Field

The recurring field of participation through which relation is carried into form — through action, material, tool, method, and craft.

## Places

Making Field places a recurring field of participation.

## Holds

Making Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], [[Making]], [[Participation]], [[Form]], and [[Carrying]]. Field of Participation must structure a recurring scope and relation must be carryable into form before the making field can be named.

## Pairs

Making Field pairs with [[Natural World Field]]. Making Field names the field of intentional form — relation carried into arrangement through action and craft; Natural World Field names the field of organic form — relation carried into arrangement through natural order without intentional design.

## Reads

Making Field becomes recognisable where producing, building, tool use, craft, or formed arrangement organises participation through what is made available.

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Form]], [[Function]], [[Tool]], [[Medium]], [[Carrying]], [[Harm]], [[Control]], and [[Hidden Carrying]] can become terminal to this field as local reads such as [[Tool]], [[Weapon]], and [[Hidden Carrying]].

## Carries

Making Field carries [[Tool]], [[Weapon]], and [[Hidden Carrying]] — the structural reads through which augmenting tools, redirected capacity, and displaced dependencies are named at this field scope. Nested reads are recorded under Nests; each traces its own structural ground.

Making Field also carries [[Gardening]] as an applied domain read — the practice through which maintained coupling holds organic asymmetry within a compatible boundary.
