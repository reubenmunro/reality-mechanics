# Knowledge Field

Knowledge Field names the recurring field of participation through which knowing, modelling, and carrying what has been retraced become readable as organised experience.

## Places

Knowledge Field places a recurring field of participation.

## Holds

Knowledge Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], [[Know]], [[Carrying]], [[Recurrence]], and [[Compatibility]]. [[Knowledge]] nests within this field; it does not hold the field that carries it.

## Pairs

Knowledge Field pairs with [[Faith Field]]. Knowledge Field names the field of carried knowing tested through recurrence and compatibility; Faith Field names the field of carried commitment toward unavailable resolution. Each requires the other to be distinguishable: knowledge tests what faith carries; faith holds what knowledge cannot yet reach.

## Reads

Knowledge Field becomes recognisable where knowing, modelling, teaching, revision, inheritance, or retraced understanding organises participation as carried knowledge.

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Know]], [[Readability]], [[Retrace Read|Retrace]], [[Check]], [[Modelling]], and [[Bearing Source]] can become terminal to this field as local reads such as [[Knowledge]], [[Competence Boundary]], [[Reality Check]], [[Teaching]], [[Teaching Boundary]], and [[Diagnosis]].
