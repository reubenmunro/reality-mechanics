# Nourishment

Nourishment names a sustenance path read where living participation receives and carries availability enough to continue.

## Places

Nourishment places the condition through which intake of compatible material renews available carrying — the structural read of compatible intake sustaining the body's or field's continued carrying.

## Holds

Nourishment is held by [[Sustenance Field]], [[Live]], [[Integration]], [[Medium]], and [[Sustenance]]. Live conditions must be present, intake must become compatibly carried through a medium, and sustenance must require renewal before nourishment can be named.

## Pairs

No lateral pair is required at this placement yet. Nourishment names sustenance through compatible intake; its structural contrast — sustenance through non-intake routes such as rest or repair — is not yet a named vault term.

## Traces

- [[Sustenance Field]]

- [[Integration]]
- [[Medium]]
- [[Sustenance]]

## Nests

Nourishment nests within the Sustenance Field as the intake-renewal condition through which compatible carrying is sustained.

## Reads

Nourishment becomes readable where what is taken in preserves or restores compatible living carrying at a closure scope.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Availability]] and [[Bearing]] make continued participation carryable.

Second: [[Live]], [[Integration]], and [[Medium]] make nourishment evaluable.

Third: [[Nourishment]] places that structural read inside the [[Sustenance Field]].
