# Sustenance Field

The recurring field of participation through which nourishment, renewal, and continued carrying become readable as conditions for sustained life and participation.

Sustenance Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], and [[Sustenance]]. [[Nourishment]] nests as a later read; it does not hold the field that carries it.

## Places

Sustenance Field places a recurring field of participation.

## Holds

Sustenance Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], and [[Sustenance]]. [[Nourishment]] nests as a later read; it does not hold the field that carries it.

## Pairs

Sustenance Field pairs with [[Life Field]]. Sustenance Field names the field of renewal conditions that keep living available; Life Field names the field of the living condition itself. Living requires sustenance to continue; sustenance is only required where something lives.

## Reads

Sustenance Field becomes recognisable where nourishment, replenishment, maintenance, or renewed availability organises participation by keeping it able to continue.

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Availability]], [[Capacity]], [[Intake]], [[Carrying]], [[Earth Carrying]], and [[Resolution]] can become terminal to this field as local reads such as [[Sustenance]] and [[Nourishment]].

## Carries

Sustenance Field carries [[Nourishment]] — the intake-renewal condition through which compatible carrying is sustained. Nested reads are recorded under Nests; each traces its own structural ground.
