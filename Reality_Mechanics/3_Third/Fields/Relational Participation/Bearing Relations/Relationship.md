# Relationship

Relationship names relation held as carried, coupled participation. It is relation given a shared boundary-form through which participants can bear on each other, maintain contact, repair, loosen, or decouple.

The "ship" read is structural rather than etymological: relationship is relation carried in a held form.

## Places

Relationship places the sustained, named form of coupled participation — the shared boundary through which bearing, repair, and ongoing relation take place.

## Holds

Relationship is held by [[Coupled Boundary]], [[Bearing Relation]], and [[Participation]]. Coupling must be bounded, bearing must be traceable across it, and participation must recur before relation can be held as a named shared form.

## Nests

Relationship nests within Relational Participation as the named form of coupled participation. It is where the second-order structure of Coupled Boundary and Bearing Relation becomes a held, readable shared condition.

## Reads

Relationship becomes recognisable where relation is held through a coupled boundary and recurring participation so that what happens between participants can be carried, strained, protected, repaired, loosened, or lost as a shared condition.
