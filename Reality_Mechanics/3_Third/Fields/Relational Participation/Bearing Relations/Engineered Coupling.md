# Engineered Coupling

Engineered coupling is compatible Coupling carried through imposed, constructed, corrected, intentionally arranged, or externally maintained carrying conditions.

## Places

Engineered Coupling places coupling that is deliberately constructed to hold distinct resolved conditions in compatible mutual availability — coupling shaped by constraint and carrying rather than emergent.

## Holds

Engineered Coupling is held by [[Coupling]], [[Carrying]], [[Constraint]], and [[Compatibility]]. Coupling must hold, carrying must continue, a constraining structure must shape the coupling, and the imposed continuation must remain compatible before it can be read as engineered.

## Pairs

Engineered Coupling pairs with [[Nested Coupling]]: engineered coupling names coupling deliberately constructed to hold distinct resolved conditions in compatible mutual availability through constraint; nested coupling names coupling held within a larger coupling structure — mutual availability that is itself carried within a containing relation. Each requires the other to be locatable — engineered coupling is only readable against the possibility of coupling arising through nesting; nested coupling is only readable against the possibility of deliberately constructed coupling.

## Nests

Engineered Coupling nests within Relational Participation as the condition through which coupling is deliberately structured — the structural ground for Technology Domain and AI domain reads.

## Reads

Engineered coupling becomes readable where constructed carrying preserves availability, bearing, resolution, and dependency order.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Bearing]] and [[Resolution]] make coupling possible.

Second: [[Coupling]], [[Carrying]], [[Constraint]], and [[Compatibility]] make arranged continuation evaluable.

Third: [[Engineered Coupling]] places that structural read inside made or externally maintained relation.
