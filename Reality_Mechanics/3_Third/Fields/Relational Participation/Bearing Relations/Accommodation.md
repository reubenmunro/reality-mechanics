# Accommodation

Accommodation is compatible bearing of variability through resolution.

## Places

Accommodation places compatible bearing of variability through resolution — the condition through which difference remains borne without requiring its elimination.

## Holds

Accommodation is held by [[Bounded Asymmetry]], [[Bearing]], and [[Resolution]]. Variability must be localised and held before it can be compatibly borne through resolution.

## Pairs

Accommodation pairs with [[Constrained Resolution]]. Accommodation names how bounded variability is borne through resolution; Constrained Resolution names the general condition of resolution within constraint that Accommodation embodies.
