# Nested Coupling

Nested coupling names Coupling that becomes available as participation in further coupling.

## Places

Nested Coupling places coupling held within a larger coupling structure — distinct resolved conditions in mutual availability where that availability is itself carried within a containing relation.

## Holds

Nested Coupling is held by [[Coupling]], [[Nested Relation]], and [[Carrying]]. Coupling must be present, the nested relation must carry it, and carrying must continue before the nesting of coupling can be read.

## Pairs

Nested Coupling pairs with [[Engineered Coupling]]: nested coupling names coupling held within a larger coupling structure — mutual availability that is itself carried within a containing relation; engineered coupling names coupling deliberately constructed to hold conditions in compatible mutual availability through constraint. Each requires the other to be locatable — nested coupling is only readable against the possibility of engineered coupling; engineered coupling is only readable against the possibility of coupling arising through nesting.

## Nests

Nested Coupling nests within Relational Participation as the condition through which coupling is carried within a wider carrying scope — the structural ground for family, organisation, and embedded social structure reads.

## Reads

Nested Coupling becomes readable where coupling participates in further coupling without losing its dependency order.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Bearing]] and [[Resolution]] make coupling possible.

Second: [[Coupling]], [[Nested Relation]], [[Participation]], and [[Carrying]] make further relation evaluable.

Third: [[Nested Coupling]] places that structural read inside bearing relations.
