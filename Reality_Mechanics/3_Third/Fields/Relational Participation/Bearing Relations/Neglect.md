# Neglect

The failure to hold bearing that preserves availability — the condition where care-bearing becomes absent, insufficient, or discontinued.

Neglect is held by [[Care]], [[Bearing]], and [[Availability]]. Care must be the relevant condition and availability must be at stake before bearing's absence or insufficiency can be named as neglect.

## Places

Neglect places the failure to hold bearing that preserves availability — the condition where care-bearing becomes absent, insufficient, or discontinued.

## Holds

Neglect is held by [[Care]], [[Bearing]], and [[Availability]]. Care must be the relevant condition and availability must be at stake before bearing's absence or insufficiency can be named as neglect.

## Pairs

Neglect pairs with [[Care]]. Care names bearing that preserves availability through relation; Neglect names the failure to hold that bearing.

## Traces

- [[Care]]
- [[Bearing]]
- [[Availability]]

## Nests

Neglect nests within Relational Participation as the condition where care-bearing fails to hold — the structural ground for repair, harm, and care-without-bearing reads.

## Reads

Neglect becomes readable where availability requires bearing and the required bearing is not supplied at the relevant closure scope.

## Carries

- [[Repair]]

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Availability]] requires [[Bearing]] to remain holdable.

Second: carrying fails because required bearing is not supplied or maintained.

Third: [[Neglect]] places that absence inside bearing relations.
