## Reads

Reciprocity Breakdown becomes readable where one side of participation continues without the mutual bearing required for reciprocal carrying.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Bearing]] must hold enough for mutual participation to remain supportable.

Second: [[Participation]], [[Compatibility]], and [[Closure Scope]] make reciprocity evaluable as mutually carried or broken.

Third: [[Reciprocity Breakdown]] tests that condition inside the [[Relational Participation Field]] as an applied relation read.

Case Movement:

```text
participation is mutual
-> carrying becomes uneven
-> one side continues without reciprocal bearing
-> reciprocity breaks down
```
