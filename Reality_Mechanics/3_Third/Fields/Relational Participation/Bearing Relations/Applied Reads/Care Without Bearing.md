## Reads

Care Without Bearing becomes readable where a relation names care while failing to carry what the other condition requires.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Bearing]] must hold enough for care to preserve availability.

Second: [[Compatibility]] and [[Closure Scope]] make care evaluable as carried support or absent bearing.

Third: [[Care Without Bearing]] tests that condition inside the [[Relational Participation Field]] as an applied relation read.

Case Movement:

```text
care is named
-> required bearing is absent
-> availability is not preserved
-> care-language loses carrying authority
```
