# Trust

The read of ongoing supportability within a relationship — that what is held between participants can continue to bear through strain without becoming unretraceable.

Trust is held by [[Relationship]], [[Bearing Relation]], and [[Recurrence]]. A relationship must exist, bearing must be traceable across it, and recurrence must show the condition holding over time before trust can be read.

## Places

Trust places the read of ongoing supportability within a relationship — that what is held between participants can continue to bear through strain without becoming unretraceable.

## Holds

Trust is held by [[Relationship]], [[Bearing Relation]], and [[Recurrence]]. A relationship must exist, bearing must be traceable across it, and recurrence must show the condition holding over time before trust can be read.

Repair remains an opening within trust rather than a prerequisite for it. Where betrayal or violation occurs, [[Repair]] may make renewed trust available, but trust does not depend on repair already having occurred.

## Pairs

Trust pairs with [[Betrayal]]. Trust names the read that a coupled boundary will hold through strain; Betrayal names when that boundary is broken against participation.

## Traces

- [[Relationship]]
- [[Bearing Relation]]
- [[Recurrence]]
- [[Coupled Boundary]]

## Nests

Trust nests within Relational Participation as a bearing-relations read. It does not replace direct retrace; it reads whether the structure for retrace remains intact through recurring participation.

## Reads

Trust becomes readable where recurring participation shows that the coupled boundary of a relationship can hold, loosen, repair, and continue without losing retraceable support.
