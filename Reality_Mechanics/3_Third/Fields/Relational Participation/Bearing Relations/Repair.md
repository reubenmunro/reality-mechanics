# Repair

Repair names compatible re-bearing where required bearing has become absent, interrupted, insufficient, or misdirected at a closure scope.

## Places

Repair places compatible re-bearing where required bearing has become absent, interrupted, insufficient, or misdirected at a closure scope.

## Holds

Repair is held by [[Strain]], [[Harm]], [[Neglect]], [[Restraint]], [[Betrayal]], [[Violation]], and [[Regulation]]. The absence, interruption, or misdirection of required bearing must be present before compatible re-bearing can be named.

## Pairs

Repair pairs with [[Violation]]. Violation names the break of a coupled boundary against compatible participation; Repair names the restoration of what the break damaged.

## Traces

- [[Strain]]
- [[Harm]]
- [[Neglect]]
- [[Restraint]]
- [[Betrayal]]
- [[Violation]]
- [[Regulation]]
## Nests

Repair nests within Relational Participation as the condition through which required bearing is restored — the structural ground for trust, misrepair, and repair-mismatch reads.

## Reads

Repair becomes readable where absent, interrupted, insufficient, or misdirected bearing becomes compatibly borne again at a closure scope, restoring the relation's ability to receive what failed to bear.

## Carries

Repair carries [[Misrepair]] — the condition where carrying proceeds while the wrong condition continues, preserving motion over correction.
