# Faith Field

The recurring field of participation through which carried commitment, mystery, sacred orientation, and unavailable resolution become readable as organised experience.

## Places

Faith Field places a recurring field of participation.

## Holds

Faith Field is held by [[Field of Participation]], [[Organic Field]], [[Faith Read|Faith]], [[Bearing]], [[Delayed Availability]], and [[Closure Scope]]. Field of Participation must structure a recurring scope and bearing must hold what cannot yet be read before the faith field can be named.

## Pairs

Faith Field pairs with [[Knowledge Field]]. Faith Field names the field of carried commitment toward unavailable resolution; Knowledge Field names the field of carried knowing tested through recurrence and compatibility. Each requires the other to be distinguishable: faith holds what knowledge cannot yet reach; knowledge tests what faith carries.

## Reads

Faith Field becomes recognisable where unavailable resolution, mystery, sacred orientation, or carried commitment organises participation without becoming source authority.

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Availability]], [[Hidden Bearing]], [[Invisible Bearing]], [[Hope]], [[Higher Faith]], and [[Boundary]] can become terminal to this field as local reads such as [[Faith Read]], [[Faith Boundary]], and [[Hope]].

## Carries

Faith Field carries [[Hope]] — the structural read through which carried commitment is held at this field scope. Nested reads are recorded under Nests; each traces its own structural ground.
