# Faith Read

The third-order field condition through which carried commitment, mystery, ritual, and sacred orientation are held without requiring full readability at the current closure scope.

Faith Read is held by [[Branch]], [[Readability]], [[Delayed Availability]], [[Bearing]], and [[Closure Scope]]. Bearing must hold what cannot yet be read, and delayed availability must be the structural condition before carried commitment can be named as faith.

Faith is above the current read, not above [[Relation]].

## Pairs

Faith Read pairs with [[Faith Boundary]]. Faith Read names carried commitment through bearing that holds under delayed availability; Faith Boundary names the edge at which that commitment meets what cannot be resolved at scope.

## Reads

Faith Read becomes readable where carried commitment remains oriented toward unavailable resolution without making what exceeds the current read into source authority.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Boundary]] and [[Bearing]] hold relation where resolution is not fully available.

Second: [[Readability]] and [[Delayed Availability]] distinguish present read from unavailable resolution.

Third: [[Faith Read|Faith]] places carried commitment, mystery, ritual, sacred orientation, [[Faith Boundary]], and [[Hope]] inside [[Faith Field]] without making them source authority.
