# Binding

Binding names constrained participation through carried influence. Binding is not constraint in general; it is influence holding participation within a narrowed course.

## Places

Binding places the constraining mode of enchantment — where influence narrows how participation can continue, exit, respond, or re-enter.

## Holds

Binding is held by [[Enchantment]], [[Constraint]], and [[Participation]]. Influence must alter readability, constraint must be operative, and participation must be present before narrowed course can be read as binding rather than as ordinary constraint.

## Pairs

Binding pairs with [[Wonder]]. Binding names enchantment constraining participation; Wonder names enchantment opening it.

## Traces

- [[Enchantment]]
- [[Constraint]]
- [[Participation]]
- [[Influence]]

## Nests

Binding nests within the enchantment cluster as the constraining path read — the specific mode where enchantment holds participation within a narrowed course rather than expanding or misrepresenting it.

## Reads

Binding becomes recognisable where influence constrains how participation can continue, exit, respond, or re-enter. The path through influence and constraint must be entered for binding to become readable rather than being mistaken for constraint in general.
