# Third Order

Third Order names participating carrying becoming organised and nested into recurring fields, domains, branches, and applied reads.

Third Order begins where participation becomes recognisable through the organisations it repeatedly forms.

In the tree read, Third Order is branching. [[Field of Participation|Fields]] are major branches of recurring participation. [[Domain|Domains]] are organised branchings within or across fields. [[Translation Boundary|Translation boundaries]] are joints where the read changes regime. [[Path Reads]] and [[Applied Read|applied reads]] are visible growth.

It also names the [[Translation Boundary|translation layer]] where structural Atlas terms become terminal to a field or domain. A root term remains retraceable, but past the branch joint the field or domain must carry it in its own terms.

Nesting is the order root of Third Order: participation remains available inside recurring organisation.

Third Order depends on second-order [[Bearing Relation]] without being reducible to it. Bearing relation lets relation participate; Third Order begins where that participation recurs into organised fields, domains, branches, and reads.

The primary Third Order distinction is between organic and organised modes of organisation: [[Organic Field|organic fields]] arise through participation; [[Organised Domain|organised domains]] intentionally, culturally, technically, institutionally, or methodically arrange participation within one or more fields.

## Places

Third Order places participating carrying becoming organised and nested into recurring fields, organised domains, branches, and applied reads. It also places the [[Translation Boundary|branch joint]] where an Atlas term becomes terminal to a field, domain, practice, or applied read without losing its trace.

## Holds

Third Order is held by [[Second Order]], [[Participation]], and [[Field of Participation]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Third Order is a structural level marker. The asymmetry it names is fully expressed by the level it carries into: [[Higher Order]].

## Nests

Third Order nests fields, domains, branches, translation boundaries, path reads, placement modes, and applied diagnosis as forms of organised participation.

Within that nesting, a structural term may become terminal to a field or domain: the Atlas trace still holds, but the next readable movement belongs to the local language of the participation.

## Reads

Third Order becomes recognisable where carrying through participation becomes organised and nested while its connections remain available as carry and trace.

It is read by asking what kind of growth a note is: field branch, organised domain branch, translation boundary, reusable path read, or applied visible read.
