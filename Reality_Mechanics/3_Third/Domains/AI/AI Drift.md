# AI Drift

The disorder condition through which generated output departs from the dependency structure it claims to carry while preserving surface coherence — the specifically generated form of Source Drift.

## Places

AI Drift places the disorder condition through which generated output departs from the dependency structure it claims to carry while preserving surface coherence — the specifically generated form of Source Drift.

## Holds

AI Drift is held by [[AI]], [[Dependency Disorder]], [[Disorder]], [[Surface]], [[Coherence]], [[Hidden Bearing]], and [[Source Drift]].

## Pairs

AI Drift pairs with [[AI Coupling]]. AI Coupling names maintained compatible joining across human read, generated output, and Atlas structure; AI Drift names the condition where that joining is lost while surface coherence persists.

## Nests

AI Drift nests within the AI domain as the specifically generated disorder pattern of Source Drift.

## Reads

AI Drift becomes readable where generated output preserves surface coherence while losing the dependency path it appears to carry.

AI Drift can also become readable through [[Extractive Acceleration]] where fast generated resolution depends on hidden drawdown of attention, source work, judgement, energy, trust, or repair. In that case the drift is not only incorrect output; it is speed treated as intelligence without retracing what carries the speed.
