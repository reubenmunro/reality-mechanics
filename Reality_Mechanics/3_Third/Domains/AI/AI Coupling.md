# AI Coupling

AI coupling names maintained Engineered Coupling between human read, machine output, Atlas structure, and evaluative proceeding.

## Places

AI Coupling places the coupling condition specific to AI interaction — where human read, generated output, Atlas structure, and evaluative proceeding remain compatibly joined so that the output can be tested and retraced.

## Holds

AI Coupling is held by [[AI]], [[Engineered Coupling]], [[Carrying]], and [[Hidden Carrying]] — it requires that the four-way joining remain compatible through the interaction.

## Pairs

AI Coupling pairs with [[AI Drift]]. AI Coupling names maintained compatible joining; AI Drift names the condition where dependency structure is lost while surface coherence persists.

## Nests

AI Coupling nests within the AI domain as the structural coupling condition that grounding and availability depend upon.

## Reads

AI Coupling becomes readable where human read, generated output, model interface, and Atlas structure remain compatibly joined through an AI interaction.

AI Coupling also clarifies responsibility. Generated output may carry consequence, but responsibility is not located in the model as an isolated object. It is traced through the human-AI or institution-AI coupled boundary: prompt, interface, deployment, domain of use, evaluation, check, action, and consequence field.

AI on its own is structurally incomplete as an actionable participant. Its actionable face appears through coupled boundary. The question is therefore not only whether AI is agent or tool, but where actionable coupling occurs and what boundary remains answerable for what follows.
