# Technology Domain

The organised participation context through which produced arrangements extend, modify, automate, or support participation.

Technology Domain is held by [[Domain]], [[Making Field]], [[Carrying]], [[Function]], [[Medium]].

## Places

Technology Domain places the organised participation context through which produced arrangements extend, modify, automate, or support participation.

## Holds

Technology Domain is held by [[Domain]], [[Making Field]], [[Carrying]], [[Function]], [[Medium]].

## Pairs

## Nests

Technology Domain nests within [[Making Field]] as a branch index. It organises sub-domains — currently [[AI]] and [[Architecture]] — rather than carrying its own Applied Reads. New sub-domains (tools, infrastructure, automation) belong under Technology Domain where they carry a distinct branch of the same organised participation structure.

## Reads

Technology Domain becomes recognisable where participation is intentionally arranged through tools, systems, techniques, infrastructure, automation, or produced capabilities.

Technology Domain also becomes recognisable where produced arrangements accelerate resolution. That acceleration remains supportable only where the order carrying the speed remains retraceable; otherwise the domain can be read through [[Extractive Acceleration]].
