# Parenting

Parenting names branch-level participation where dependent life is carried through care, boundary, teaching, repair, and ongoing relation.

## Places

Parenting places branch-level participation where dependent life is carried through care, boundary, teaching, repair, and ongoing relation.

## Holds

Parenting is held by [[Branch]], [[Care]], [[Teaching]], [[Compatibility]], [[Recognition Read]].

## Pairs

Parenting pairs with [[Teaching]]. Teaching names knowledge-bearing through medium for a participant who can already carry; Parenting names care-bearing through recognition and compatibility for a participant whose carrying capacity is still developing. Each is a distinct developmental bearing condition.

## Reads

Parenting becomes readable where dependent participation is borne through care, boundary, repair, and learning at the relevant closure scope.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Availability]], [[Boundary]], and [[Bearing]] make dependent participation carryable.

Second: [[Compatibility]] and [[Recognition Read|Recognition]] make care, repair, and learning evaluable.

Third: [[Parenting]] places that structural read inside relational participation across body, society, knowledge, and our-story.

Applied reads:

- [[Parenting Boundary]]
- [[Parenting Repair]]
- [[Protective Control]]

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Care]], [[Boundary]], [[Teaching]], [[Repair]], [[Compatibility]], and [[Recognition Read|Recognition]] become terminal to this domain as [[Parenting Boundary]], [[Parenting Repair]], and [[Protective Control]]. In the other direction, Parenting becomes terminal to the [[Relational Participation Field]] as [[Parenting as Maintained Coupling]].
