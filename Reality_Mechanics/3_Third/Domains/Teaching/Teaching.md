# Teaching

Teaching names branch-level participation that preserves and develops available learning conditions through compatible uptake.

## Places

## Holds

## Pairs

Teaching pairs with [[Learning]] and [[Parenting]]. Teaching carries bearing toward the learner through medium; Learning names what becomes available through that bearing. Parenting names care-bearing and developmental holding beside teaching's knowledge-bearing.

## Reads

Teaching becomes readable where instruction carries a learner into available uptake without imposing order faster than the learner can bear.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Asymmetry]], [[Availability]], and [[Bearing]] make a learning condition carryable without pretending teacher and learner bear the same read.

Second: [[Readability]], [[Recognition Read|Recognition]], [[Know]], [[Medium]], and [[Compatibility]] make teaching evaluable as compatible uptake rather than mere instruction.

Third: [[Teaching]] places that structural read within knowledge participation.
