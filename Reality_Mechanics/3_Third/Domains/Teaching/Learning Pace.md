# Learning Pace

Learning pace names how learning participation remains compatibly carried through a teaching condition without exceeding available bearing.

## Places

Learning Pace places how learning participation remains compatibly carried through a teaching condition without exceeding available bearing.

## Holds

Learning Pace is held by [[Teaching]], [[Bearing]], [[Carrying]], [[Tempo]], [[Pace]].

## Pairs

Learning Pace pairs with [[Teaching Boundary]]. Learning Pace names the supportable rate at which bearing can be received; Teaching Boundary names the edge where that rate can no longer hold.

## Reads

Learning Pace becomes readable where instruction proceeds at a rate the learner can still carry, test, and re-enter.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Bearing]] holds learning availability.

Second: [[Carrying]] and [[Compatibility]] make pace evaluable through learner uptake rather than teacher momentum.

Third: [[Learning Pace]] places that structural read inside teaching.
