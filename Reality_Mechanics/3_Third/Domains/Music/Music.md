## Nests

Music nests within [[Expression Field]] and [[Body Field]] as a domain branch. Its local reads are musical terms, but their support remains retraceable through time, space, rhythm, recurrence, medium, pattern, and carrying.

## Reads

Music becomes readable where relation is carried through rhythm, timing, sound, silence, recurrence, variation, and embodied participation.

The dependency movement is:

First: [[Time]], [[Space]], [[Bearing]], and [[Resolution]] must hold enough for recurrence, silence, sound, and variation to become readable.

Second: [[Medium]], [[Pattern]], and [[Carrying]] make musical participation evaluable.

Third: [[Music]] places that carried expression inside the [[Expression Field]] as a domain branch.

At the [[Translation Boundary]], [[Time]], [[Space]], [[Musical Rhythm]], [[Recurrence]], [[Medium]], [[Pattern]], and [[Carrying]] become terminal to this domain as timing, sound, silence, recurrence, variation, and embodied participation.
