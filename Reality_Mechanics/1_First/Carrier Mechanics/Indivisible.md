# Indivisible

Non-separable distinction — distinction that cannot be separated without changing the condition being read.

## Places

Indivisible places non-separable distinction.

## Holds

Indivisible is held by [[Boundary]]. Distinction must be located before non-separability can be read.

## Pairs

Indivisible pairs with [[Divisible]]. Divisible carries separability; Indivisible carries non-separability without sameness.

## Traces

- [[Boundary]]

## Nests

Indivisible nests as a structural carrier. It can support later indivisible bearing without making non-separability into sameness.

## Reads

Indivisible becomes recognisable where distinction is read as unable to separate without changing the condition under evaluation.
