# In

In carries interior placement: relation readable as within a bounded condition. Possession or enclosure can involve in, but in itself carries inward placement into later compounds such as within, inside, input, inhabit, and in-relation.

## Places

In places interior relation within a bounded condition.

## Holds

In is held by [[Boundary]]. A boundary must locate distinction before interior placement can be read.

## Pairs

In pairs with [[Out]]. In carries interior placement; Out carries exterior placement.

## Traces

- [[Boundary]]

## Nests

In nests as a structural carrier that can enter later compounds such as within, inside, input, inhabit, and in-relation.

## Reads

In becomes recognisable where relation is read as within a located condition.

- [[Within]]
