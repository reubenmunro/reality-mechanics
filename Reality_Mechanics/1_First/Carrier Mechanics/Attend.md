# Attend

Attend names sustained presence with what has been noticed. Where [[Notice]] names first contact with what has become apparent, and [[Enter]] names passage into the relation opened by that contact, Attend names the chosen staying-with through which relation remains available long enough to become followable, readable, or cared for.

Attend comes before judgment, closure, or explanation. It keeps contact open without forcing the condition to resolve before its difference can be read.

## Places

Attend places sustained presence with what has been noticed and entered.

## Holds

Attend is held by [[Notice]], [[Enter]], and [[Availability]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward. Attend is a singular carrier primitive — the condition through which notice becomes directed. There is no lateral contrast required at the attend level. The asymmetry is expressed in what attend makes possible: [[Trace]], [[Read]], and [[Readability]].

## Traces

- [[Notice]]
- [[Enter]]
- [[Availability]]

## Nests

Attend nests between first contact and trace. It keeps relation open before judgment, closure, or explanation.

## Reads

Attend becomes recognisable where a noticed distinction is held in contact long enough for its relation, difference, or path to begin disclosing itself.
