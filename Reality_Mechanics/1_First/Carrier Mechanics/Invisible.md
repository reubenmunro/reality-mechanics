# Invisible

Non-presented readability — distinction that can hold without being presented to read.

## Places

Invisible places non-presented readability.

## Holds

Invisible is held by [[Apparent]]. A distinction can be apparent to structure without being presented to read.

## Pairs

Invisible pairs with [[Visible]] and differs from Hidden. Invisible can be background-carrying; hidden implies concealment or blocked read.

## Traces

- [[Apparent]]

## Nests

Invisible nests under apparent presentation as its non-presented counterpart. It can remain structurally active without becoming absent from relation.

## Reads

Invisible becomes recognisable where distinction is read as not presented rather than absent from relation.

## Carries


- [[Invisible Bearing Read]]
