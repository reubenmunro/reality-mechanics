# Resolved Asymmetry

Resolved asymmetry names strained asymmetry determined at the current scope: settled enough to remain distinguishable without collapsing into sameness. Where [[Strained Asymmetry]] names the condition under contrast, resolved asymmetry names that condition after determination. It establishes the determined condition through which carrying and coupling can become available.

## Places

Resolved Asymmetry places strained asymmetry determined at the current scope — settled enough to remain distinguishable.

## Holds

Resolved Asymmetry is held by [[Strained Asymmetry]]. Contrast must be present before it can settle without collapsing into sameness.

## Pairs

Resolved Asymmetry pairs with [[Strained Asymmetry]]: strained asymmetry names the condition under contrast; resolved asymmetry names that same condition settled into determination at the current scope.

## Traces

- [[Strained Asymmetry]]

## Nests

Resolved Asymmetry nests at the asymmetry crossing toward Second Order. It makes carrying available.

## Reads

Resolved asymmetry becomes recognisable where strained asymmetry can be read as determined at scope — where contrast has settled into a distinguishable condition without dissolving the asymmetry that made distinction possible.
