# Clear

The readable side of clean relation — where relation is unobstructed enough for clearance to be held.

Clear is held by [[Clean]] and [[Relation]]. Relation must remain free from residue or drift before it can be read without obstruction.

Clear is not the same as [[Clean]]: Clean names the invariant carrier of undrifted relation; Clear names the readable condition that Clean makes available.

Held clear offers [[Clearance]], [[Relation Read]], and [[Daylighting]]. Clearance is held separation that can be read — not merely empty space.

## Places

## Holds

## Pairs

Clear pairs with [[Tact]]. Clear governs readability; tact governs handling at contact.

## Traces

- [[Relation]]
- [[Clean]]

## Nests

Clear nests before [[Clearance]]. It is the readability condition from which held separation can become readable as clearance.

## Reads

Clear becomes recognisable where relation can be read without obstruction, obscurity, or false mixture.

- [[Clearance]]
- [[Relation Read]]
- [[Daylighting]]
