# Space

Held clearance as separation and extent — relation readable as position, distance, here/there, co-presence, and placement.

Space is held by [[Clearance]]. Relation must remain held apart enough to become locatable.

## Places

Space places held clearance as separation and extent.

## Holds

Space is held by [[Clearance]]. Relation must remain held apart enough to become locatable.

## Pairs

Space pairs with [[Time]]. Space reads clearance as separation and extent; Time reads clearance as interval and continuation.

## Traces

- [[Clearance]]

## Nests

Space nests before boundary and before later reads of empty container or absolute extension. It names relation as placed rather than only persisting.

## Reads

Space becomes recognisable where clearance can be read as separated, extended, or located relation — where differences are not just held apart but placed in relation to each other. In later structure, space can carry as the unoccupied or uncarried interval that lets relations align and become readable.
