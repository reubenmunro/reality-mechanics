# Relation Read

Relation Read names what becomes readable through clearance: relation as joined spread under a shared condition. Where [[Clearance]] names held separation, relation read names what that separation makes available before any local asymmetry is selected.

## Places

Relation Read places the readable face of [[Clearance]].

## Holds

Relation Read is held by [[Clearance]] and [[Clear]]. Held separation must keep relation readable, and relation must be clear enough to be read as joined spread.

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward into [[Readability]]. Relation Read names the readable face of [[Clearance]]: relation as joined spread before local asymmetry is selected.

## Traces

- [[Clearance]]
- [[Clear]]

## Nests

Relation Read nests before local boundary selection. It reads spread under a shared condition without yet selecting one asymmetry from it.

## Reads

Relation Read becomes recognisable where a condition can be taken up as spread rather than as a located distinction — where the join is present but no boundary has yet selected one asymmetry from it.

## Carries



- [[Readability]]
