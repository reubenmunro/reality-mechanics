**What First Order makes available.**

Relation becomes held and directionally available as carry and trace. Place makes that availability enterable, readable, and checkable. Boundary, availability, strain, bearing, and resolution can locate and shape the conditions that pass through connection.

**The crossing.**

Where [[Carry]] is enacted, [[Carrying]] becomes available as the first second-order movement. Resolution may shape a particular crossing, but it is not required to define every instance of carrying.
