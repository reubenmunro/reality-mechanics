# Threshold (First → Second)

This threshold names the crossing from First Order into Second Order.

## Places

Threshold (First → Second) places the crossing where held first-order carry is entered and enacted as second-order carrying.

## Holds

Threshold (First → Second) is held by [[First Order]], [[Hold]], [[Connection]], [[Carry]], and [[Place]].

## Pairs

Asymmetry carries downward — marks the transition into [[Carrying]] and [[Second Order]]

## Nests

Threshold (First → Second) nests between orders. It is not a general threshold; it marks the specific passage from first-order determination into second-order carrying.

## Reads

Threshold (First → Second) becomes recognisable where held forward availability is entered or continued as carrying.

**What First Order makes available.**

Relation becomes held and directionally available as carry and trace. Place makes that availability enterable, readable, and checkable. Boundary, availability, strain, bearing, and resolution can locate and shape the conditions that pass through connection.

**The crossing.**

Where [[Carry]] is enacted, [[Carrying]] becomes available as the first second-order movement. Resolution may shape a particular crossing, but it is not required to define every instance of carrying.
