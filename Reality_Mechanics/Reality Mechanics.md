# Reality Mechanics

Reality Mechanics names the [[Discipline]] of keeping relation traceable as order. It maps terms by retraceable dependency: what holds them, what they carry, what they pair with, where they trace, how they read, and where they can be corrected.

It begins from a working postulate: relation holds, order carries, trace places. Order becomes answerable where relation can hold, what holds can carry, and what carries can be traced back.

It is the practice through which relation becomes readable, readability becomes traceable, traceability becomes answerable, and answerability returns to practice. Terms, experiences, corrections, and contributions are placed, checked, revised, and grounded according to immediate support, readable condition, and available openings.

The [[Atlas]] is the dependency-ordered reasoning system produced through this discipline. Its written notes are the current worked and tended surface. The Atlas is not the authority-object; the relation-order-trace practice is what must remain repeatable.

## Places

Reality Mechanics places the discipline of keeping relation traceable as order through a working postulate, grounded retrace, accumulated carrying, and answerable placement.

## Holds

Reality Mechanics is held by [[Ground]] and the working root named by [[Theory]]: it begins from what is acknowledged before the system derives its own terms.

## Pairs

Reality Mechanics pairs with [[Atlas]]. Reality Mechanics is the discipline; Atlas is the worked and tended surface produced through the discipline.

## Reads

Reality Mechanics becomes recognisable where a term can be placed by structural role, direct trace, readable condition, available openings, and retraceable correction without losing dependency order.

It also becomes recognisable where a domain read can be translated outward into its own language and back inward into the Atlas without hiding the dependency that made the read possible.
