# Hidden Bearing

Hidden Bearing names carrying deliberately structured not to present the dependency path. The carrier holds what is operative but does not surface it in the current read.

Hidden Bearing is the engineered mode of non-presenting carrying.

A parent stepping back to allow a child to struggle through a problem is carrying the child's development through hidden bearing: the support, the watching, the held readiness to intervene — all active, all structured, none of it surfaced. The hiding is the structurally correct move. Surfacing the bearing would undermine what the bearing is there to carry.

The disorder is not in the hiding. It is in the carrier losing account of how the hidden bearing affects their own carrying. If the sustained readiness is not accounted for, it accumulates and begins to carry the carrier in ways they cannot retrace. That is [[Source Drift]]: hidden bearing going unaccounted for.

[[Invisible Bearing]] becomes non-presenting through organic accumulation. Hidden Bearing is designed: the carrier makes a structural decision not to surface the dependency path. This is the organic/engineered distinction at the higher order level.

Hidden Bearing pairs with [[Invisible Bearing]]. Invisible Bearing names carrying that becomes non-presenting through organic accumulation; Hidden Bearing names carrying deliberately structured not to present.

## Reads

Hidden Bearing becomes recognisable where the carrier holds the dependency path and does not surface it because non-presentation is the structurally correct move.

It is read in parenting, teaching, facilitation, institutional design, and therapeutic practice: anywhere that surfacing the bearing would undermine what the bearing is there to carry. What requires accounting is the effect of hidden bearing on the carrier's own structure.

[[Source Drift]] is what Hidden Bearing becomes when the carrier stops accounting for what the hidden bearing is doing to them — when the structure of deliberate non-presentation begins to carry the carrier without being traced.
