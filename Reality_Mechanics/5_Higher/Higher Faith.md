# Higher Faith

Higher Faith names participation through carrying that has repeatedly resolved through higher retrace, remaining locatable against the possibility of Source Drift — trust that retrace would not confirm. Where invisible bearing names nested organisation carrying participation without requiring explicit retracing, higher faith names the condition through which that carrying is trusted to continue: the dependency path has been retraced enough times that participation can move forward without reconstructing it each time. Faith here is not belief without trace. It is participation carried by what retrace has repeatedly confirmed. Faith is above the current read, not above Relation.

## Places

Higher Faith places participation through carrying that has repeatedly resolved through higher retrace.

## Holds

Higher Faith is held by [[Invisible Bearing]] and [[Higher Retrace|Retrace]].

## Pairs

Higher Faith pairs with [[Source Drift]]. Higher Faith names participation through carrying that retrace has confirmed answerable; Source Drift names participation through carrying that is taken as source without retrace. Each requires the other: Higher Faith is only nameable against the condition where trust is unfounded; Source Drift is only nameable against the condition where trust would be grounded.

## Reads

Higher Faith becomes recognisable where participation continues through a carrying condition that has been retraced enough times to be trusted — where the dependency path is not reconstructed but remains available if needed.
