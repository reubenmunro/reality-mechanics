# Higher Recognition

The coherence of derivation and observation within the same read.

Higher Recognition is held by [[Retrace Read|Retrace]] and [[Observation]].

## Places

Higher Recognition places the coherence of derivation and observation within the same read.

## Holds

Higher Recognition is held by [[Retrace Read|Retrace]] and [[Observation]].

## Pairs

Higher Recognition pairs with [[Higher Learning]]. Higher Learning names participation becoming able to change its own future carrying through conscious retrace; Higher Recognition names the coherence of derivation and observation that makes that self-correction possible. Each requires the other: Recognition without the capacity to act on it is passive; Learning without the coherence to see what it is changing is blind.

- [[Observation]]

## Nests

Higher Recognition nests where recognition holds across nested fields, domains, and orders.

## Reads

Higher Recognition becomes recognisable where the dependency path through which something became available and the direct encounter of it as present are the same read — where tracing back and looking directly arrive together.
