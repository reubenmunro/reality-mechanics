# Self-Regulation

Higher-order carrying used to adjust, inhibit, repair, preserve, or redirect participation.

Self-Regulation is held by [[Invisible Bearing]], [[Self-Modelling]], [[Regulation]], and [[Compatibility]].

Self-Regulation can be organic where nested bearing adjusts participation without imposed modelling.

Self-Regulation can be engineered where a self-model, rule, method, institution, or practice is used to regulate carrying.

## Places

Self-Regulation places higher-order carrying used to adjust, inhibit, repair, preserve, or redirect participation.

## Holds

Self-Regulation is held by [[Invisible Bearing]], [[Self-Modelling]], [[Regulation]], and [[Compatibility]].

## Pairs

Self-Regulation pairs with [[Self-Modelling]]. Self-Modelling reads the self; Self-Regulation adjusts carrying through that read.

- [[Invisible Bearing]]
- [[Self-Modelling]]

## Nests

Self-Regulation nests within higher-order or practice conditions.

## Reads

Self-Regulation becomes recognisable where participation adjusts its own carrying in response to what has become readable.

It is read by asking whether the regulation preserves retraceability or becomes a hidden bearing that maintains itself.
