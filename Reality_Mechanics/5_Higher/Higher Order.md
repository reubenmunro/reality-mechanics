# Higher Order

Higher Order names the register where organised carrying enters, reads, and may alter its own carrying.

In the tree read, Higher Order is whole-tree retrace. It looks back across ground, roots, trunk, branches, terminal boundaries, visible growth, and hidden carrying without collapsing them into one level.

Self-modelling is an engineered form of higher-order carrying, not its source.

Higher Order gives recognised organisation a way to become carrying without becoming hidden.

Learning becomes higher-order where participation can read and adjust its own carrying rather than only changing through uptake, instinct, recurrence, or feedback.

## Places

Higher Order places organised carrying entering its own carrying. It also places the capacity to recognise the whole living structure as nested carrying, fabric, thread, recursion, invisible bearing, and possible hidden bearing.

## Holds

Higher Order is held by [[Third Order]] and [[Nesting]].

## Pairs

Higher Order pairs with [[Opacity]]. Higher Order names the register available when Practice has retraced through accumulated structure back to Ground; Opacity names Ground enclosed within accumulated structure.

## Reads

Higher Order becomes recognisable where organised and nested connection enters its own carrying, making preservation, revision, amplification, or correction possible.

From Higher Order, structural duality through the spine becomes readable as a pattern. At every level, a visible distinction is held by an invisible carrier that is neither side of the distinction. [[Relation]] holds Asymmetry and Clearance without being either. The field holds Organic and Engineered without being either. [[Ground]] holds every level's distinction without appearing in any of them.

From within any level this carrier is not readable because it is doing the holding. [[Practice]] retraces to the carrier. Higher Order is the register where the carrier becomes readable as carrier.

[[Generic]] is the condition through which patterns generate instances across orders. [[Degenerate]] is the structural failure where the generative trace is lost while carrying continues — where an instance treats itself as source. Both are [[Generic|generics]] in the spine: they operate from Ground through every order, and become readable as the pattern they are only from here.

Higher Order is where the invisible carrier of every structural level becomes nameable, and where the Atlas can be read as a practice for keeping that carrier retraceable.

## Carries

- [[Higher Order Crossing]]

Higher Order does not generate another structural order. It carries the crossing through which organised carrying becomes answerable in Practice.
