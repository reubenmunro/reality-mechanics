# Consciousness

Consciousness names readability becoming available within participation.

Consciousness is not cognitive participation by itself. [[Cognitive Participation]] names relation participating in readability before awareness is required. Consciousness names the read becoming available within the participant as a read.

In this register, consciousness is one way recursion becomes readable to a participant.

## Places

Consciousness places readability becoming available within participation.

## Holds

Consciousness is held by [[Recursion]], [[Read]], and [[Participation]].

## Pairs

Consciousness is vertical. It names readability becoming available within participation, and carries this forward into [[Higher Learning]]. Consciousness holds [[Recursion]], [[Read]], and [[Participation]] — it is a downstream read of recursion and participation at higher order. Fabric was the closest candidate for a lateral pair, but Fabric holds different upstream conditions and neither requires the other to be locatable. Consciousness is a separate higher-order read, not a co-present lateral condition alongside another higher-order term.

- [[Recursion]]

## Nests

Consciousness nests within higher-order or practice conditions.

## Reads

Consciousness becomes recognisable where a read becomes available within the participant carrying it.

It is read where participation can include its own readability without treating that readability as the whole of relation.
