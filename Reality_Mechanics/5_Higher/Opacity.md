# Opacity

The structural condition where accumulated third-order carrying has rendered Ground unretraceable from within — where the dependency chain still runs to Ground but can no longer be entered from the participant's current position without disciplined practice.

Opacity is held by [[Third Order]], [[Nested Carrying]], [[Invisible Bearing]], and [[Ground]]. Third-order participation must have accumulated, Invisible Bearing must have layered past available Higher Retrace, and Ground must be present but enclosed.

Opacity names the point where [[Invisible Bearing]] has accumulated past the threshold at which [[Higher Retrace]] can enter without [[Higher Order Crossing|disciplined practice]]. The structure continues to carry. Ground continues to bear it. But the participant cannot reach what bears them from within what they carry.

## Nests

Opacity nests within the Higher Order register as the terminal condition that makes Higher Order Crossing structurally necessary — the hinge between accumulated participation and the Practice that can re-establish contact with Ground.

## Reads

Opacity becomes readable where accumulated participation has reached a depth from which Ground can no longer be retraced from within: the structure carries the participant more than the participant carries the structure.

The dependency movement:

First: [[Third Order]] — participation has accumulated across fields while remaining traceable in principle.

Second: [[Nested Carrying]] — each order carries within and through the prior, so the dependency chain reaches back through what has accumulated.

Third: [[Invisible Bearing]] — accumulated structure carries participation without presenting itself. Opacity begins where this has accumulated past the threshold at which Higher Retrace can enter it from the participant's current position.

Fourth: [[Ground]] — Ground has not disappeared. Opacity is the condition where this remains true and unreachable at once.

[[Higher Order Crossing]] names the threshold that becomes structurally necessary when Opacity holds — the entry into disciplined Practice through which the accumulated structure can be retraced back to what has been bearing it. Practice does not create new Ground; it finds what was already there inside the enclosure.

The circuit closes here: Ground bears accumulation; accumulation produces Opacity; Practice retraces Opacity back to Ground; [[Higher Order]] names the register where Ground becomes readable within accumulated participation.

## Carries

Opacity carries nothing further as a structural source. What follows from Opacity is not a branch but a crossing.
