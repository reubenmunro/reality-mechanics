# Commissions

## Purpose

This register records commissions issued by the Project Steward and their current state.

A commission begins with pressure and authorises a contract or architectural response.

This is not a task list.

---

## Commission States

| State | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Open** | Commission has been named but not resolved |
| **Contracted** | A contract has been issued |
| **Resolved** | Pressure has been carried enough for a steward decision to stand |
| **Rejected** | Commission was not accepted or no longer applies |
| **Continued** | Pressure remains and further work is required |

## Notes

A resolved commission does not mean all related work is complete.

It means the named pressure has been carried far enough that its current relation is known and the steward can decide what follows.
