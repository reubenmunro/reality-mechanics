## Pass 3 — Retrace

**Can an independent reader return to every statement through dependency?**

Downstream terms (Carry, Trace, First Order Crossing) depend on Connection. Connection depends on Relation only within first order.

```text
Relation (primitive)
    ↓ needs / holds / traces
Connection (passage, bidirectional)
    ↓ carries
Carry (forward) · Trace (backward) · First Order Crossing
```

## Pass 5 — Language

### Rules applied

- Preserve meaning  
- Remove unsupported wording  
- Strengthen retrace (Relation before Carry/Trace)  
- Strengthen dependency clarity  
- Reduce interpretation  
- Never invent ontology  

### Original text (opening body, lines 29–36)

```markdown
# Connection

Connection names relation holding between distinguishable conditions.

Within its own note, connection comes before its directional reads; it is not primary to the working root. Forward, held connection offers [[Carry]]. Backward, it offers [[Trace]]. Carry and trace do not make two connections; they are directional availability within one connection.

Connection does not require a participant to read it. A participant may enter the forward availability as [[Carrying]] or follow the backward availability through [[Retracing]], but neither act creates the connection.

Where there is no connection, no carry or trace is claimed between the conditions. Absence of connection is not automatically failure, drift, or disorder.
```

### Calibrated text (applied)

```markdown
# Connection

Connection names relation holding between distinguishable conditions so that passage is available in more than one direction.

Connection is held by [[Relation]]. Relation must remain held before connection within relation can be read.

Held connection offers [[Carry]] forward and [[Trace]] backward. Carry and trace are directional availability within one connection, not two separate connections.

Connection does not require a participant to read it. A participant may enter the forward availability as [[Carrying]] or follow the backward availability through [[Retracing]], but neither act creates the connection.

Where there is no connection, no carry or trace is claimed between the conditions. Absence of connection does not by itself name [[Failure]] or [[Disorder]].
```

### Change log (structural justification)

| Change | Structural justification |
|---|---|
| Opening sentence extended with **“so that passage is available in more than one direction”** | Aligns opening with `conditions.places` — names the place completely in one retraceable sentence; removes need for interpretive supplement |
| **Removed** “Within its own note…working root” | “Working root” is not retraced within Connection’s needs/holds/traces; replaced with explicit **Relation hold** (same structural fact, retraceable) |
| **Added** “Connection is held by [[Relation]]…” before Carry/Trace | Dependency order preserved: Relation → Connection → Carry/Trace (matches `needs`, `holds`, `traces`) |
| **Reordered** Carry/Trace sentence to name terms first | Forward/backward movement retraces to carried terms immediately |
| **Removed** third repetition of “do not make two connections” phrasing | Pairs field already states this; one clear sentence retained |
| **Failure / Disorder** wikilinked; **drift** removed | Failure and Disorder are grounded Atlas terms; drift is not — removes unsupported ontology hint |
| Participant and absence paragraphs | **Preserved** — already name place and negative read without interpretation |

`## Places` through `## Carries` template sections **unchanged** — they mirror frontmatter by convention; calibration targeted interpretive opening prose.

## Experimental read — Place and Movement

Not a score. A relation.

```text
Place
──────
Movement
```

| State | Original opening | Calibrated opening |
|---|---|---|
| **Characterisation** | **Movement obscures place** | **Movement faithfully reveals place** |
| **Evidence** | Carry/Trace named before Relation hold; “working root” and document-order language interpret where they should name | Relation hold precedes directional reads; opening aligns with `places`; unretraceable language removed |

When movement faithfully reveals the place, language becomes sensible.

When movement obscures the place, language becomes interpretive.
