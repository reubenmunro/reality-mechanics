# Reality Mechanics Mission

## Purpose

**Reality Mechanics exists to increase structural perception.**

Reality already carries order.

Humans participate within that order.

Participation becomes more faithful when the questions we ask, the operations we perform, and the answers we preserve remain retraceable.

Reality Mechanics develops the disciplines and instruments that make this possible.

Its purpose is not to invent structure.

Its purpose is to increase our ability to perceive, recover, preserve, and communicate the structure that is already there.

## Mission

Every part of the project serves this mission.

- Atlas preserves structural answers.
- Practice disciplines structural questions.
- Calculus preserves order through transformation.
- Calibration measures the quality of participation.
- Theory explains why the discipline works.
- Proof demonstrates retraceability.
- The Observatory reveals structure that cannot otherwise be reliably perceived.

Every contribution should strengthen structural perception.

Every correction should improve retraceability.

Every instrument should help participants perceive reality more faithfully.

## Compass

Before adding a feature, document, visualisation, AI worker, or term, ask:

> Does this increase structural perception?

If not, it does not yet serve the mission.
